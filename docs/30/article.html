1. Why This Exists

Streaming can be rewarding — but it can also bring pressure, stress, and exposure to unhealthy online behavior.

NOIZ exists to champion a different approach. We intentionally designed systems that encourage:

  • Positive, inclusive communities
  • Creator resilience and sustainable engagement
  • Platform-led moderation that prioritizes wellness

You are never expected to “just deal with it.” If something feels off, support is built into the foundation of the platform.

2. In-Platform Tools for Emotional Safety

For Creators (Stream Settings):

  • Blocklists and keyword filtering (custom word filters per channel)
  • Mod-only chat modes (temporarily restrict posting to trusted users)
  • Stream delay options (helpful in high-stress or high-risk streams)
  • Anonymity toggles for viewer boosts or gifting milestones

For Viewers:

  • Anonymous viewing mode (no public logs of presence)
  • Option to hide their leaderboard/gift contribution visibility
  • Toggle quest tracking (pause contribution display if desired)

3. How to Set Boundaries as a Creator

You control your environment. Setting tone early helps.

Ways to establish boundaries:

  • Add clear rules in your channel description and “First Join” popup
  • Use Quests to reinforce expectations (e.g. “Be kind, stay chill”)
  • Include panel overlays or bot messages with your comfort preferences
  • Empower your mods and editors — they can act on your behalf if needed

If things feel too heavy:

  • Take a break mid-stream (pause quests, turn off alerts)
  • Disable chat temporarily
  • Hide viewer stats or engagement triggers for a session

4. NOIZ Community Culture: Momentai

Inspired by Mixer’s “CAT” bot, NOIZ uses this term to represent intentional calm and balance.

You’ll encounter the “Momentai” concept across the platform in forms like:

  • Optional low-stimulation overlays (reduced movement, fewer alerts)
  • Momentai Bot: auto-messages during emotionally flagged events
  • Calm Mode triggers: disable all stream animations/emote spam in one toggle

These tools aren’t punitive. They are here to help you maintain clarity and control, without judgment or penalty.

5. When to Ask for Help

You don’t need to justify discomfort to anyone.

Reach out or use built-in tools if:

  • You’re facing repeated harassment or targeted raids
  • You feel overwhelmed, anxious, or panicked during a stream
  • A viewer situation feels “off,” uncomfortable, or manipulative
  • You’re uncertain about whether to report someone

You can take action by:

  • Enabling a “Wellness Alert” on your dashboard — this pings your mod team and, if escalated, DCT
  • Temporarily disabling quests or viewer interactivity
  • Using report tools to flag unsafe behavior (streamers OR viewers)

7. NOIZ’s Role

We aren’t counselors — but we do everything we can to protect the emotional space of creators and viewers alike.

We will:

  • Build features that reduce stress and overstimulation
  • Respond to reports of abuse or predatory behavior swiftly
  • Respect your choice to step back or slow down — no punishment, no guilt

We will not:

  • Ignore mental health concerns reported through our systems
  • Penalize creators for disabling engagement tools during rough days
  • Encourage grind culture or performance pressure

8. FAQ

Q: Can I turn off all alerts during a rough day?
A: Yes — “Momentai Mode” and alert toggles allow full stream quieting.

Q: Can I use quests to share mental health resources?
A: Absolutely. Creators are welcome to offer wellness-themed quests with safe messaging.

Q: What if I’m worried about another creator?
A: Submit a quiet flag using “Concern for Another Creator” — this will route your note to the DCT with no public log.

Q: Can I request extra mod help during hard times?
A: Yes. You can tag your stream as a Wellness Watch session — this allows Staff or DCT to silently monitor for safety violations or targeted harassment.
