Viewer rewards on NOIZ are cosmetic, digital-only unlockables earned through Quests, milestones, or streamer interaction. They never include real-world value and are safely stored in your profile inventory.

1. What Are Collectible Viewer Rewards on NOIZ?

Viewer rewards are cosmetic digital items earned through:

  • Quests
  • Viewer milestones (like Vibe or ⚡︎dB support)
  • Sponsored campaigns
  • Event-specific achievements
  • Manual grants by streamers (via Reverb or plugins)

These rewards are:

  • Non-transferable
  • Stored in each viewer’s Profile Inventory
  • Often visible in chat or on stream panels

2. Types of Viewer Rewards

TypeDescription
🏅 BadgesDisplayed in chat; includes time-based, Vibe, gift, Quest, and event types
🎨 EmotesUnlockable global or channel emotes from quests or sub levels
📦 Overlay UnlocksVisual effects or plugin themes earned during special campaigns
🧩 Reverb RewardsChannel point redemptions (e.g., "highlight my message")

3. How Streamers Can Grant Rewards

Streamers configure custom unlocks via:
Dashboard → Interactivity → Viewer Rewards

They can:

  • Create Reverb-based redemptions (channel point equivalents)
  • Assign Quest-based badges
  • Launch limited-time event unlocks
  • Tie rewards to:
    • ⚡︎dB tip levels
    • Gift sub milestones
    • Plugin triggers

All streamer-created rewards:

  • Must remain cosmetic-only
  • Are subject to branding, maturity, and TOS moderation

4. Viewer Progression Systems

Viewer rewards align with multiple systems:

  • Quest chains
  • Vibe milestones
  • Gift sub counts (5, 10, 25… 5,000)
  • Channel loyalty (1m to 10y time badges)

Badge types include:

  • 🎯 Personal Progress – shown in profile
  • 💬 Chat Badges – shown next to username
  • 🛠 Functional Unlocks – used via Reverb or plugins

5. Event & Sponsored Rewards

During platform events or brand-sponsored quests:

  • Viewers unlock exclusive, time-gated cosmetics
  • Some react dynamically to plugins (e.g. flash when a hype goal is met)
  • Most are tied to specific games or streamers

Sponsored reward rules:

  • Must be pre-approved
  • Always show expiration dates
  • May not include physical goods or money-equivalent prizes

6. Storage and Display

All unlocked rewards go to:
Profile → Inventory → Viewer Rewards

Viewers can:

  • Equip up to 3 badges (static or rotating)
  • Preview unlocked emotes, overlays, collectibles
  • Filter rewards by:
    • Quest
    • Streamer
    • Platform
  • View progress:
    e.g., “3/10 Vibe badges earned”

7. FAQ

Can streamers give rewards manually?
→ Yes — via Reverb actions or Quest plugins

Can I trade or gift my rewards?
→ No — all viewer rewards are non-transferable

Can I lose rewards?
→ Only if removed for policy violations or if they expire

Can rewards be used in chat?
→ Yes — most show up as badges or unlock custom emotes
