All Marketplace assets on NOIZ require clear licensing so creators, streamers, and the platform are protected.

1. Why Licensing Matters on NOIZ

To protect:

  • Creators from having their assets misused or redistributed
  • Streamers from using content that gets flagged or taken down
  • NOIZ from hosting improperly licensed or third-party material

All Marketplace assets must include clear disclosure about:

  • Who made it
  • What rights the unlocking user receives
  • Whether any elements are from third-party sources (with permission)

2. Default Licensing Framework

When an asset is listed:

  • The uploader retains full copyright
  • NOIZ receives a non-exclusive, platform-limited license to distribute it via the Marketplace
  • Unlockers (streamers or viewers) receive a personal-use license valid only on NOIZ
