What Are Chat Roles on NOIZ?

On NOIZ, chat roles are visual indicators that help identify a user’s status, trust level, or platform function. These badges appear in chat messages, user profiles, channel dashboards, and leaderboards. Roles serve cosmetic and functional purposes, highlighting contributors, moderators, builders, or official representatives.

There are two main types of roles:

  • Automatic roles – earned through platform activity (e.g. Affiliate, Sub Gifter, Quest Participant)
  • Assigned roles – granted manually by NOIZ staff or system triggers (e.g. DCT, Staff, Pathweaver, Ambassador)

Users can have multiple roles, but only one badge is shown in chat at a time; the highest‑trust or platform‑critical badge takes priority.

Automatic Roles Displayed in Chat

These roles are granted automatically based on activity or support milestones.

RoleTrigger
AffiliateCreator has met monetization entry criteria and completed verification.
PartnerCreator meets higher trust and engagement thresholds; badge displayed by default.
Partner+Legacy or standout creators with high-level privileges; badge shown in most views.
Sub GifterViewer who gifts Decibel-based subs; badge color changes with total gifted (5 to 5000).
Vibe Badge HolderBadge visible only in the creator’s chat; earned through accumulated Vibe.
Support MilestoneTime-based or gifting milestones like 1 year subscribed or 1000 dB gifted.

Gifter and Vibe badges stack separately from monetization badges. In your own channel, your time-based badge is shown by default unless manually changed.

Role Display Priority & Context

  • In their own chat, users display their time-based loyalty badge.
  • In another creator’s chat, the platform-level badge (Affiliate, Partner, Partner+) is shown.
  • Staff, Admin, or DCT badges override all others when acting in an official capacity.

Badge switching or customization may become available depending on context and permissions.

Future Role Expansions

NOIZ plans to expand the role system with additional badges:

  • Verified Artist – for creators with listed Marketplace assets
  • Verified Developer – for users who build approved plugins, overlays, and tools
  • Pathweaver – for users who operate trusted relays and support platform infrastructure
  • Quest Champion – for high quest engagement across campaigns

These roles will follow the same color-coded badge structure and appear automatically once earned.

FAQ

  • Can I choose which badge shows in chat?
    Platform badges show by default; in your own chat, the time-based badge is prioritized.
  • What if I have multiple roles?
    The highest-priority badge is displayed automatically. Some creators may unlock badge-switching later.
  • Can viewers see if someone is Staff or DCT?
    Yes, when the user acts in an official capacity, though staff may observe anonymously.
  • Can I earn multiple badges at once?
    Yes, users can accumulate gifting, time-based, Vibe, Partner+, and other badges.
  • Are there secret roles?
    Some moderation roles are hidden, but all chat-visible roles are standardized.
