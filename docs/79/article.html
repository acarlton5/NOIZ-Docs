This guide explains how artists earn, track, and withdraw ⚡︎dB from their creations on NOIZ while keeping everything framed as virtual goods.

1. How Artists Earn on NOIZ

Artists can earn ⚡︎dB through:

  • Viewer unlocks of assets (emotes, overlays, audio, etc.)
  • Commissions (when enabled for Verified Artists)
  • Sponsored placement allocations (e.g. for featured asset packs)
  • Quest integrations (e.g. viewers unlock your work through campaigns)

⚡︎dB is NOIZ’s charged virtual token — similar to Twitch Bits — and must be earned through platform engagement, not purchased directly for payout.

2. Unlock-Based Earnings

Each listing you publish can be:

  • Unlocked by viewers for ⚡︎dB (you set the unlock amount)
  • Offered as a Quest reward (NOIZ sets terms and handles reward fulfillment)
  • Included in promotional bundles (with shared ⚡︎dB allocation)

You’ll receive:

  • A platform allocation of ⚡︎dB from each viewer unlock of your listing
  • The ⚡︎dB allocation percentage varies based on your Artist Tier (Creator / Verified / Featured)

⚠️ Faded (non-cashable) dB cannot be earned through Marketplace unlocks.

3. Commission Earnings (Verified Only)

Verified Artists can accept:

  • ⚡︎dB commissions from streamers or viewers
  • Marketplace-bound commissions (delivered as unlockables)
  • Private or public commissions (you choose listing visibility)

⚠️ All commissions must remain digital-only — no off-platform transactions or physical goods are allowed.

4. Viewing and Managing Earnings

Your Artist ⚡︎dB allocations can be tracked from:
Dashboard → Artist Hub → ⚡︎dB Earnings

There you can:

  • View ⚡︎dB allocations by listing
  • See unlock logs (anonymized viewer IDs)
  • Break down ⚡︎dB by listing, bundle, or event
  • Export logs for recordkeeping or support

5. Cashing Out

Only charged ⚡︎dB (earned through viewer interaction, not purchases) can be cashed out.

To initiate a withdrawal:

  1. Accumulate at least 10,000 ⚡︎dB (≈ $100 USD equivalent)
  2. Open:
    My Loot → ⚡︎dB → Cashout
  3. Select payout method (regional options will vary)

⚠️ Marketplace ⚡︎dB is eligible for cashout as long as it comes from verified platform activity (not refunded, looped, or internally boosted).

6. Using Earnings On-Platform

⚡︎dB earned from your work can also be used to:

  • Support other creators
  • Unlock premium plugins or overlays
  • Participate in Quests
  • Access Marketplace items
  • Gift subs and boost stream visibility

Artists are not required to cash out — reinvesting your ⚡︎dB within the platform is fully supported.

7. Payout Schedule

Artist ⚡︎dB follows the standard cashout cycle:

  • Monthly review period for integrity checks
  • Net-30 payout window (after request is approved)
  • Payouts may be delayed if flagged for Quest abuse, duplicate listings, or suspicious unlock activity

8. FAQ

Can I get paid in real money?
Only if you meet the cashout threshold with charged ⚡︎dB
Do I pay a fee?
A small processing fee may apply depending on payout method
Can I cash out faded dB?
No — only charged ⚡︎dB earned through verified activity
Can streamers send me ⚡︎dB directly?
Yes — but those count as personal support, not a Marketplace unlock allocation
