Hosting lets you showcase trusted creators directly on your channel while you're offline — but it comes with responsibility.

1. What Is Hosting on NOIZ?

Hosting displays another streamer’s live broadcast on your own channel page when you’re not live.

  • Your chat stays active
  • Your overlays and branding remain visible
  • The hosted stream’s video content replaces your player area

It’s best used for:

  • Supporting trusted creators
  • Featuring teammates or collaborators
  • Keeping your channel engaging while offline

Hosting is not Raiding.
Hosting embeds someone else’s stream on your page. Raiding sends your viewers to theirs.

2. How Hosting Works

When you activate Hosting:

  • Your stream box shows the hosted streamer’s feed
  • Your chat stays open (unless disabled)
  • Your own overlays stay visible (can be hidden with a plugin)
  • Any ⚡︎dB tips go to the hosted streamer, not you
  • Your analytics reflect host mode time and viewer behavior

You can host:

  • ✅ Manually: /host @username or via UI toggle
  • ✅ Automatically: Auto Host List settings
  • ✅ As part of official campaigns like Echostage relays

3. Hosting Responsibility

🛑 You are fully accountable for anything that happens during host mode.

Hosting is a form of endorsement — it’s your name, your chat, and your visibility.

If you host someone who breaks the rules:

  • You may receive moderation penalties
  • ⚡︎dB earnings or Partner/Echostage priority can be frozen
  • You’re expected to vet creators on your Auto Host List

If you're not 100% sure about the creator you're promoting — use Raiding instead. Hosting = trust.

5. How to Host on NOIZ

To start hosting:

  1. Go to Dashboard → Channel Settings → Hosting
  2. Enable hosting mode
  3. (Optional) Add creators to your Auto Host List
  4. Use /host @username in chat or dashboard
  5. Use /unhost to return to your offline screen

You can configure:

  • Host rotation rules
  • Cooldown timers
  • Maturity tag filters (avoid mismatched content ratings)

6. Hosting Etiquette & Risk Prevention

✅ Do:

  • Vet everyone on your host list
  • Match stream maturity tags (Mature ↔ Mature)
  • Rotate hosts to support more creators
  • Let streamers know if they’re being hosted regularly

❌ Don’t:

  • Host banned users or delisted content
  • Host content that violates NOIZ’s rules
  • Use hosting to redirect to unsafe or exploitative content
  • Embed other platforms’ live feeds via overlay (prohibited)

7. FAQ

Can I earn ⚡︎dB while hosting?
→ Not directly. Viewers can still interact in chat and tip, but no Quest or ⚡︎dB milestones trigger for you during host mode.

Can I restrict who gets hosted automatically?
→ Yes — use filters, cooldowns, and maturity tag matching.

What if the hosted stream breaks TOS?
→ You are liable. DCT treats it as if it aired on your stream.

How is this different from Raiding?
→ Raiding transfers viewers to another streamer’s page. Hosting embeds their content into your page — so you take on full responsibility.
