What Is “My Loot”?

My Loot is your personalized dashboard that displays creator earnings and platform engagement rewards. It serves as your hub for reviewing:

  • ⚡︎dB earned through subscriptions, gifts, tips, and boosts
  • Campaign rewards and quest completions
  • Marketplace or plugin unlocks tied to your content

💡 “Loot” represents charged Decibels (⚡︎dB) earned from platform participation. It is not fiat currency, not payroll, and not a financial account.

⚡︎dB vs dB Overview

Type Source Cashout Eligible Appears In “Loot”?
dB Purchased by viewers ❌ No Viewer-side spend activity
⚡︎dB Earned via support actions ✅ Yes (if eligible) Creator reward & tracker

🔎 You will only see ⚡︎dB totals in your cashout and reward panels. Faded dB (viewer-purchased) are never eligible for withdrawal and are not listed as your earnings.

Loot Dashboard Panels

Your dashboard includes:

  • ⚡︎dB Balance — current eligible amount for use or cashout
  • Pending ⚡︎dB — earned from streams still under validation or active campaigns
  • dB Spend Log — how viewers supported your channel
  • Gifting Activity — subs received, grouped by viewer
  • Quest Impact — breakdown of viewer completions and reward triggers
  • Boost Streaks — timelines for Reverb/dB-based boost chains

Payout Eligibility Overview

This panel helps you understand where you stand for cashout:

  • ⚡︎dB total vs. 10,000⚡︎dB minimum
  • Eligibility status (Affiliate and KYC completed?)
  • Payout request status and next cycle timeline
  • Notices for tool visibility (hidden until Affiliate level reached)

🪙 NOIZ does not automatically convert your ⚡︎dB — you control when to request cashout.

How to Read Your Activity Feed

Each ⚡︎dB you earn is tracked in a filterable feed:

  • Action Source (tip, sub, quest, plugin, etc.)
  • Timestamp (date + time)
  • Related Stream or Item (which session or Marketplace listing)

Filters available include:

  • Tips
  • Subscriptions
  • Gifts
  • Campaign Quests
  • Marketplace
  • Boosts

Privacy & Viewer Support

You control how support is displayed:

  • Hide ⚡︎dB totals from overlay and chat announcements
  • Mute gift alerts during stream events or quests
  • Show top supporters using built-in overlay plugin (optional)

🔐 All visibility settings are adjustable per stream.

What Loot Is Not

Let’s clarify what My Loot isn’t:

  • ❌ Not a bank account
  • ❌ Not a wallet
  • ❌ Not a financial service or earnings guarantee

“Loot” represents platform-issued recognition — not money, not employment, not contractual payout.

FAQ

Why can’t I see a payout button?
You may not have reached Affiliate yet or haven’t earned 10,000 ⚡︎dB.
What if my ⚡︎dB isn’t updating?
Check if it’s in “Pending” from a recent stream or campaign. Some rewards take up to 72 hours to confirm.
How do I report a wrong transaction?
Go to the NOIZ Support Portal and submit a ticket with your transaction ID and description.
Do I get notified when I reach 10,000 ⚡︎dB?
Yes — a notification appears in your dashboard and optionally in your stream overlay (if enabled).
