Understanding Stream Ratings & Maturity Labels

Why Stream Ratings Matter

NOIZ uses stream ratings to:

  • Help viewers make informed choices
  • Ensure safe browsing experiences for all ages
  • Maintain platform compliance with global content standards

Ratings also assist with stream discovery, tagging, and enforcement of community safety. Creators are responsible for correctly labeling their streams based on content, presentation, and chat dynamics.

Failure to use accurate ratings may result in warnings or visibility restrictions from the platform.

NOIZ Stream Rating Categories

Everyone
General content suitable for all ages. No mature themes, swearing, or innuendo. Safe for classrooms, family-friendly spaces, or viewers under 13.
Teen
Some mild language, suggestive themes, or fantasy violence. No graphic depictions, adult language, or intense imagery. Recommended for viewers 13+.
Mature
May contain frequent strong language, intense topics, mature attire, or suggestive content. Viewer discretion is advised. Use this tag for adult audiences or streams that explore real-world topics with complexity. Adult-themed content may fall under this category — but this does not permit pornographic, overtly sexual, or indecent content. NOIZ does not allow pornographic or explicit adult content in any form, as outlined in our Terms of Use.

Best Practices for Stream Ratings

  • If in doubt, choose the higher rating to avoid issues.
  • Use Mature rating for streams with:
    • Revealing clothing or intense flirtatious roleplay
    • Heavy profanity or graphic storytelling
    • Innuendo that may not be suitable for younger audiences
  • Avoid baiting the rating system (e.g., labeling a stream “Everyone” while allowing mature discussion or behavior).
  • If your chat behavior shifts during stream (e.g., lots of adult jokes or commentary), consider updating your label.

Does Rating Affect Monetization?

  • No. Mature-rated streams are still eligible for all monetization tools, quests, and boosts.
  • Some quests or sponsored campaigns may have specific maturity rating requirements.
  • Reverb and Vibe activity are not limited by rating type.

FAQ

Can I be penalized for forgetting to change my rating midstream?
If it happens once and is quickly corrected, you’ll likely just get a system notice. Repeated mislabeling may result in enforcement review.
Can Mature streams feature sexual themes or topics?
Only to a suggestive or narrative degree. Nudity, sexual simulation, or pornography is never allowed, even under Mature.
Can I change ratings on the fly?
Yes, through your Creator Hub → Stream Settings. The system will prompt viewers with a warning when changing to Mature.
Does rating affect visibility?
Not by default. However, viewers can filter streams based on content level, and some tags (like “Family-Friendly”) will deprioritize Mature streams.
