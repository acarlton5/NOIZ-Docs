Overview

The NOIZ Marketplace is a platform for creators, artists, and developers to share digital assets in exchange for support through Decibels (dB). All digital assets on NOIZ are unlocked through support contributions and are granted as non-exclusive, non-transferable licenses for in-platform use. These assets are not "purchased" in the traditional sense and no ownership is transferred.

Key Terminology

  • Unlock: The act of gaining access to an asset by supporting the listing creator with Decibels (e.g. "Unlock this overlay for 500 dB").
  • Support-Based Licensing: Users contribute Decibels to unlock the right to use a digital asset; this is not a sale.
  • Asset Creator: The original uploader of a digital asset (emote, overlay, plugin, audio, etc.) who retains all rights unless explicitly waived.

Terms of Use for Unlocked Assets

  1. License Scope

    When a user unlocks a Marketplace item using Decibels, they are granted a limited, revocable, non-exclusive license to use that item only within the NOIZ platform.

  2. Permitted Use
    • Use in streams, overlays, audio layers, or channel visuals on NOIZ
    • Access through your Creator Dashboard
    • Use in plugins, themes, or chat features if allowed by the asset type
  3. Prohibited Use
    • Downloading for off-platform use (unless specifically enabled by creator)
    • Resale, redistribution, or uploading to other platforms
    • Claiming authorship or altering copyright markings
    • Exporting assets for personal or commercial use beyond NOIZ
  4. Asset Ownership
    • Unlocking does not transfer ownership or copyright
    • All rights remain with the original asset creator
    • NOIZ may remove listings that infringe upon third-party rights
  5. Revocation of Access

    Access to unlocked assets may be revoked in cases of:

    • Platform violations
    • Chargebacks or refund abuse
    • Legal takedown requests
    • Policy updates requiring new licensing terms

Artist & Developer Rights

  • Creators retain full control over their listings
  • Listing prices (in dB) can be adjusted at any time
  • Creators can report unauthorized use for enforcement

Enforcement & Disputes

  • Violations of this policy may result in suspension of monetization or access
  • Marketplace disputes may be handled via the NOIZ support system
  • Repeat violations may trigger DCT review or permanent removal of assets

FAQ

  • Can I download and use unlocked assets on another platform?
    No. Assets are licensed for use on NOIZ only unless explicitly marked otherwise by the creator.
  • What happens if the artist deletes the listing?
    You will retain access to the unlocked version unless the asset is removed due to a platform or legal policy violation.
  • Is unlocking considered a payment or purchase?
    No. It is a support-based action that grants licensed use of a digital good.
  • Can I refund Decibels used to unlock an item?
    No. All Decibel contributions are considered support and are non-refundable unless part of a verified policy violation.
