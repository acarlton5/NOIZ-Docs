Local Moderators (Per-Channel)

Local mods are streamer-appointed users with moderation access only within your channel. They can:

  • Time out or ban viewers from your chat
  • Delete messages
  • Flag issues with mod-only highlights
  • Monitor Quest activity and viewer boosts (read-only or via plugin)

Mod Behavior Expectations

All mods (global and local) must:

  • Moderate fairly, without personal bias or abuse
  • Avoid public shaming or retaliation
  • Respect user privacy ‚Äî mods cannot see wallets, balances, or personal info
  • Escalate serious cases through the proper channels

FAQ

  • Can mods see ‚ö°Ô∏édB balances or user wallets?
    No. Mods have no access to personal financial or support data.
  • Can I give mods access to Quest tools?
    Only if you're using a Quest plugin that supports it. Otherwise, mods can view but not edit quest settings.
  • Can banned users appeal?
    Yes. Use the Appeals Tool inside the Report menu. Global bans are reviewed by DCT.
  • What if a mod is misusing their power?
    Remove them immediately and submit a report. NOIZ investigates all mod-related misconduct.
