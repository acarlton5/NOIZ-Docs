What This Code Covers

This policy defines:

  • Behavioral expectations for relay operators (Pathweavers)
  • Ethical and compliant use of node access
  • Platform boundaries for communication, branding, and public representation
  • Terms under which relays may be adjusted, suspended, or rewarded

Hosting a NOIZ relay node is a privilege granted under operational policy, not an automatic right. This code exists to protect the reliability and trust of NOIZ’s streaming infrastructure.

Expected Conduct

As a Pathweaver and relay host, you agree to:

  • Operate your node with stability, uptime, and security as priorities
  • Maintain respectful, constructive communication with NOIZ staff and other hosts
  • Avoid misrepresenting your relay as “official” or “staff-run” unless explicitly invited to do so
  • Follow the published technical documentation exactly — no modified binaries or unsupported configs
  • Respond promptly to moderation or performance concerns flagged in your dashboard
  • Remember that your relay directly impacts viewer and streamer experience

Unacceptable Behavior

Relay operators may be suspended or blacklisted for:

  • Hosting unstable or intentionally degraded relays
  • Selling, renting, or offering “priority routing” to any third party
  • Manipulating payloads or routing to redirect viewers to off-platform services
  • Harassing other operators, streamers, or NOIZ staff
  • Spamming chats, Discord servers, or streams while displaying a Pathweaver badge
  • Misrepresenting ⚡︎dB rewards as guaranteed income or threatening platform escalation over allocations

Node Identity & Branding Rules

Allowed:

  • Assigning a public relay name (e.g., SoCal Node – ghostrelay)
  • Appearing on the Public Node Map (opt-in)
  • Displaying your Pathweaver badge in chat and on your profile

Not Allowed:

  • Using NOIZ logos in relay-host branding without approval
  • Claiming to be a NOIZ-operated or NOIZ-staff relay
  • Creating social accounts that imply official representation of the NOIZ relay network

Rewards Are Performance-Based

Relay support in ⚡︎dB is:

  • Allocated at NOIZ’s discretion based on performance metrics, uptime, and regional impact
  • Subject to change without notice for budget adjustments, routing changes, or abuse prevention
  • Calculated through internal systems, not negotiable on request

Attempts to falsify traffic, pressure staff for payout, or threaten escalation will result in immediate blacklisting.

Accountability & Trust

Your conduct as a Pathweaver reflects on the platform:

  • Assist other hosts when possible
  • Report bugs, exploits, or abuse via the dashboard — not public channels
  • Maintain a clean, current configuration
  • Avoid becoming a routing bottleneck or security risk

Multiple minor violations over time may still result in loss of Pathweaver eligibility.

Termination Clause

NOIZ reserves the right to:

  • Suspend or permanently remove a node from routing
  • Withhold rewards for fraudulent, abusive, or non-performing relays
  • Cease communication with hosts engaging in harassment, spam, or disruption
  • Update this Code at any time, with notice provided in your dashboard

FAQ

  • Can I advertise my relay? → Yes, as long as it’s neutral and does not imply staff affiliation.
  • Can I appeal a suspension? → Yes, via the Node Dashboard, unless marked permanent.
  • Can I run my relay under a company name? → Only if pre-approved and not branded as “NOIZ.”
  • Can I keep my Pathweaver badge if I’m blacklisted? → No — badges are revoked automatically when a node is removed from the program.
