Refund Philosophy on NOIZ

NOIZ operates using Decibels, a platform-specific virtual support system. All actions involving Decibels — including subscriptions, tips, boosts, and marketplace unlocks — are framed as digital support or platform interaction, not sales.

Because of this framing:

  • Support actions are non-refundable, as they do not constitute the exchange of goods or services in the traditional sense.
  • Refunds are limited to technical or policy violations — never for regret, misuse, or change-of-mind scenarios.

Refunds may be granted in specific cases such as:

  • Unauthorized Decibel unlocks (e.g., account compromise)
  • Duplicate charges or confirmed technical failures
  • Rule-breaking behavior in Marketplace or Sponsored Quest systems

What Is Not Eligible for Refund

To preserve the integrity of the support model, the following items cannot be refunded under any circumstance:

  • Subscriptions (500 dB) – All subscriptions are a form of support and include no tangible service or entitlement
  • Tipping or boosting a creator – These are voluntary, non-transactional actions
  • Gifted subs – Once gifted, support is delivered and cannot be retracted
  • Quest contributions – Unless tied to a verified system failure or a revoked Sponsored Quest
  • Completed dB unlocks – If Decibels were successfully delivered to your account, the transaction is considered fulfilled
  • Reminder: support is not ownership. There is no guarantee of content delivery, access, or performance when using Decibels.

Eligible Refund Situations

You may be eligible for a refund in limited and specific cases, such as:

  • Unauthorized dB unlocks made via a compromised Constellation account
  • System failures that result in:
    • Duplicate charges
    • Decibels not being delivered after a confirmed transaction
  • Marketplace violations, including:
    • Unlocking a stolen, duplicated, or misrepresented asset
    • Receiving an asset that was later removed for policy violations
  • Sponsored Quest fraud, such as:
    • Being directed to complete an action under false pretenses
    • Incomplete or revoked campaigns that were improperly executed

Each case is manually reviewed by NOIZ staff.

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund review, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the NOIZ Support Portal or your Constellation Transaction History
  2. Locate the transaction in question
  3. Click “Report an Issue”
  4. Submit a ticket including:
    • The transaction ID
    • The date and time of the issue
    • A brief description of the situation
  5. Refund requests are typically responded to within 3–5 business days, depending on volume and investigation needs.

Refunds for Marketplace Issues

If you unlocked an asset from the Marketplace and believe it was fraudulent or broken:

  1. Submit a Marketplace Support Ticket through your dashboard
  2. Include the asset name, creator name, and the issue (e.g., asset doesn’t load, was removed, or violates a rule)
  3. If the asset was unlocked using dB, a refund may be issued in dB
  4. If ⚡︎dB was used in error (e.g., through a system bug), platform staff may manually intervene
  5. False or abusive refund reports will result in your account being flagged, with potential limits on future refund eligibility.

Chargebacks and Fraud Protection

Chargebacks filed directly with your financial provider (e.g., credit card) bypass NOIZ’s dispute system and can have serious consequences:

  • Automatic account restriction or ban
  • Loss of access to pending ⚡︎dB or Marketplace tools
  • Required KYC re-verification for future monetization eligibility

If you're unsure or concerned about a transaction, always open a ticket first — our team can help resolve issues faster and more safely than your financial provider.

Refund Method & Timing

  • Most refunds will be returned as dB to your NOIZ account
  • In rare or complex system issues, refunds may be re-routed through your original funding method
  • In cases of technical failure, missing Decibels may be auto-restored without a formal refund process

Refund timelines can vary depending on the funding processor, but most are completed within 5–10 business days once approved.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Can I refund a gifted sub I didn’t mean to send?
    No. All gifted subs are treated as final digital support and are non-refundable.
  • What if someone tipped me accidentally?
    Support actions like tipping are voluntary and irreversible. If someone sends dB or ⚡︎dB to your channel, it becomes eligible support.
  • Do refunds affect streamer ⚡︎dB earnings?
    In very rare cases, yes. If a refunded transaction included Decibel support and fraud is verified, ⚡︎dB may be removed from the recipient’s account.
  • Can I refund my own Marketplace listing unlock?
    Only if the asset is found to violate Marketplace policy (e.g., stolen artwork). Buyer remorse or creative mismatch is not grounds for a refund.
