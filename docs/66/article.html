How Hype Moments & Boosts Work on NOIZ

1. What Is a Hype Moment?

A Hype Moment is a short, high-energy burst that marks a spike in community engagement — not just passive viewership.

It’s triggered when your stream gets loud:

  • Viewers are reacting, vibing, tipping, or completing quests
  • Chat is flying, overlays are firing, clips are being made
  • The community isn’t just watching — they’re doing

Think of it as a signal boost for real-time momentum. It’s how NOIZ surfaces streams that are alive, not just ones with big numbers.

2. What Triggers a Hype Moment?

Hype Moments can be automatically triggered or manually flagged when energy peaks.

🔁 Auto-Triggered:

  • Sudden chat surges, Vibe spam, or Reverb spikes (in 5–10s windows)
  • A burst of ⚡︎dB tips or gifted subs
  • Plugin milestones (e.g. boss kills, goal events, stream overlays reacting)
  • Quest completions or hype-focused plugin triggers
  • Viewers hitting milestones (1M Vibes, top supporter unlocks)

🔘 Manually Triggered:

  • Streamer clicks “⚡︎ Mark Hype” from dashboard or Stream Deck
  • Editors/mods tag a moment from the live Hype Panel
  • A clip gets rapid reactions (votes, shares, chat replies)

3. What Happens During a Hype Moment?

  • A custom overlay appears (e.g. “⚡︎ HYPE INCOMING”)
  • Plugins may glow, animate, or react
  • A clip is created automatically — viewers and streamer both see it
  • You get temporary discoverability boosts in:
    • Trending Clips
    • Echostage
    • Quest feeds
    • Vibe leaderboards

A Hype Moment usually lasts 10–15 seconds and doesn’t interrupt gameplay.

4. What Makes Hype Actually Matter?

Here’s the real power:

Hype affects your Hype Score — NOIZ’s dynamic engagement ranking system.

Your Hype Score is what decides:

  • Your placement in Echostage (our discovery system)
  • Your ranking in Quests or event feeds
  • How often your stream is surfaced to new viewers
  • Eligibility for some Quest campaigns or spotlight slots

And here’s the kicker:

A streamer with 500 highly engaged viewers will outrank someone with 1,500 idle lurkers.
Because at that point, you're not a community — you're a TV show.

5. Streamer Tools for Hype

  • ✅ Toggle auto-detection: Settings → Stream → Hype Detection
  • 🎨 Customize overlays, animation, and audio
  • 👥 Let trusted mods manually trigger hype
  • 📼 Review past moments in Dashboard → Hype Log

6. Best Practices

  • ✅ Encourage reactions during big stream moments
  • ✅ Use it to push clips, raids, follows, or sub pushes
  • ✅ Recognize your active community — they're what power Hype
