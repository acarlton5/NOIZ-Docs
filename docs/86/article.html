How Rewards Work

NOIZ rewards Pathweavers using ⚡︎Decibels (charged) from a platform-supported pool — not generated tokens.

Relay nodes that:

  • Stay online consistently
  • Handle real viewer traffic
  • Improve streaming latency or reliability

…are acknowledged via support-based incentives, similar to how viewers cheer streamers or streamers earn through Quests.

What Contributes to Reward Payouts

Your node’s eligibility is calculated from:

  • 📡 Uptime score – how often your relay is available
  • 🚀 Performance tier – amount of traffic handled + latency stability
  • 🌐 Regional impact – underserved region coverage may earn boosts
  • 🧩 Event support – assisting during spikes, campaigns, or Echostage routing

⚠️ Nodes that spoof activity, drop packets, or repeatedly fail handshakes are automatically flagged and excluded from rewards.

Performance Tiers

Tier Requirements Notes
🔹 Base Relay 90% uptime, serves minimum regional traffic Eligible for monthly ⚡︎dB support pool
🔸 Optimized Node Low latency, stable throughput, supports Hype Moments Larger ⚡︎dB rewards + dashboard badge
💠 Hero Route Trusted long-distance relay for core events Eligible for campaign bonuses
🛡️ Verified Relay Manually approved node in infrastructure whitelist Used for staff or premium routing only

Performance tier is calculated automatically each week and displayed in your Node Dashboard.

Support Pool Allocation

NOIZ allocates a portion of platform reserves for:

  • ⚡︎dB distribution to qualifying nodes
  • Platform routing improvements
  • Hardware incentive campaigns (optional)

This system is not tied to viewer spending — it’s an internal infrastructure reward pool, similar in spirit to how partner incentives work on other platforms.

⚠️ Faded dB (purchased tokens) cannot be converted into relay credit. Only charged ⚡︎dB from platform support is eligible for cashout under existing thresholds.

Reward Timeline

  • Rewards are calculated weekly, distributed monthly
  • Pending ⚡︎dB is visible at: Dashboard → My Node → Relay Rewards

Bonus boosts may be given for:

  • Participation in high-load events
  • Geographic uniqueness
  • Longstanding contribution (Pathweaver milestones)

Milestone Badges

Long-term relay operators can unlock:

  • 🥉 Bronze Pathweaver – 3 months uptime
  • 🥈 Silver Relay Guardian – 6 months uptime
  • 🥇 Gold Backbone – 1+ year verified uptime

These badges are visual only and not tied to increased payouts, but they signify major infrastructure contributions.

FAQ

  • Can I request higher rewards? → No — tiers are automated and internally reviewed
  • Can I view my node’s real-time rank? → Yes — under Relay Performance in your dashboard
  • Can I cash out faded dB earned from purchases? → No — only ⚡︎dB from actual support activity is eligible
  • What if I unplug my node mid-month? → Downtime affects tier eligibility for that reward cycle
