1. What Is Constellation?

Constellation is NOIZ’s secure identity and token access system — acting as the central bridge between your NOIZ account, Decibel balances, and platform-linked tools.

Rather than using usernames and passwords, NOIZ uses code-based handshakes and device-based authorization to authenticate your identity and maintain session control.

⚠️ Constellation is not a wallet and not a crypto product. It is a private identity keychain and platform ledger viewer.

Constellation handles:

  • Account login and identity authentication
  • Session linking between your device and NOIZ
  • Tracking of both dB (faded Decibels) and ⚡︎dB (charged, earned Decibels)
  • Secure access to platform features like monetization, Marketplace, and KYC tools

2. How Linking Works

To connect your NOIZ account to Constellation:

  1. Visit NOIZ and request a connection token
  2. Open Constellation App
    • If you're new, Register:
      • Paste the token and generate a 6-digit handshake code
    • If you’re already linked, Authorize:
      • Select NOIZ under “Active Connections” and generate a new handshake code
  3. Return to NOIZ
  4. Paste your 6-digit code to complete the sync

✅ Once completed, your NOIZ account is cryptographically tied to your Constellation identity — no passwords, usernames, or emails needed.

3. What Constellation Tracks

Constellation provides a clear record of your identity and digital token interactions within the NOIZ ecosystem, including:

  • dB: Your purchased (faded) Decibel balance
  • ⚡︎dB: Your earned (charged) support tokens eligible for cashout
  • Platform Connections: Your active NOIZ-linked accounts
  • Marketplace Activity: Listing history, asset unlock logs, and Creator support (if applicable)

4. Custody and Safety

Constellation does not behave like a wallet. You do not “hold” tokens personally — you view and manage them via a secure, platform-maintained ledger.

  • Decibels are not stored locally or in a personal wallet
  • There are no “seed phrases,” crypto keys, or private credentials
  • Your Decibel balances exist within the NOIZ platform, viewable and controlled through Constellation

All balances (dB and ⚡︎dB) are ledgered by the platform and remain protected until used or redeemed.

5. Requesting Withdrawals (⚡︎dB Only)

To request a cashout of earned Decibels:

✅ Requirements:

  • Be Affiliate tier or higher
  • Hold ≥ 10,000 ⚡︎dB
  • Complete KYC identity verification
  • Pass platform review for fraud/security compliance

Once eligible:

  • Visit Creator Dashboard → ⚡︎dB → Cashout
  • Confirm withdrawal destination
  • Track processing via payout status panel

❌ dB (faded) cannot be withdrawn — only ⚡︎dB earned through legitimate user support.

6. Security Tips

To keep your account safe:

  • Enable device locking in the Constellation app
  • Never share your handshake code with others
  • Use 2FA (two-factor authentication) in Constellation for payout and KYC tools
  • Monitor session history in Active Connections and log out of old devices when needed

7. What Constellation Is Not

To prevent confusion with financial products:

  • ❌ Not a crypto wallet
  • ❌ Not a bank
  • ❌ Not an exchange
  • ❌ Not a payment processor

It is simply a platform-specific identity tool that enables creators and viewers to safely use Decibels and access monetization features within NOIZ.

You do not need Constellation to just spend dB — it’s only required for managing support, listings, cashouts, and creator tools.

8. FAQ

Q: What if I lose my device?
A: You can reauthorize your Constellation account from a new device by verifying your identity and revoking the previous session.

Q: Can I link other platforms through Constellation?
A: Currently, Constellation is used only for NOIZ. Future integrations may expand to additional creator tools.

Q: Can I cash out from Constellation directly?
A: No. Cashouts are initiated via the NOIZ Creator Dashboard and processed through our payout provider, not through Constellation.

Q: What if my dB and ⚡︎dB look different?
A: This is normal. dB = what you've purchased and spent. ⚡︎dB = what you’ve earned through support. Only ⚡︎dB is eligible for cashout.
