1. What Is Constellation?

Constellation is NOIZ’s official identity and virtual token access system. It acts as your secure link to NOIZ without relying on usernames, passwords, or external login systems.

Constellation is used to:

  • Authenticate your identity using a code-based handshake
  • Track your Decibel balances (both faded and charged)
  • Manage your platform-linked accounts and session history
  • Authorize tools like payouts, marketplace access, and creator-level features

Note: Constellation is not a wallet, bank, or crypto product — it functions as a platform-specific keychain, tied to NOIZ and its internal systems.

2. How Linking Works

Connecting NOIZ to your Constellation identity is required to stream, earn, and access platform tools.

Step-by-step

  1. On NOIZ, request a connection token
  2. Open your Constellation app
    • If new: paste the token and generate a 6-digit handshake code
    • If already linked: select NOIZ from Active Connections and generate a code
  3. Enter the handshake code back on NOIZ
  4. Your account will sync — no usernames, no passwords

This pairing process ensures secure, device-based identity linking that cannot be spoofed or hijacked via traditional credential phishing.

3. What Constellation Tracks

Constellation serves as your personal ledger interface for NOIZ. It displays:

  • dB (purchased Decibels — support tokens)
  • ⚡︎dB (earned Decibels — eligible for cashout if verified)
  • Linked platform accounts (e.g., NOIZ, marketplace, relay participation)
  • Support activity logs (gifts, tips, earnings, payouts)
  • Marketplace activity (if you list assets or plugins)

All balances are view-only, meaning Decibels cannot be traded, sent, or exported off-platform.

4. Custody and Safety

You do not hold Decibels in a custodial or financial wallet. Instead:

  • All dB and ⚡︎dB remain ledgered and stored by NOIZ
  • The Constellation app simply displays your balances and activity
  • There is no crypto-like control (no seed phrases, private keys, or transfer tools)
  • You cannot move Decibels to other platforms or convert them into other tokens

This system protects creators and viewers by maintaining platform-contained security and compliance.

5. Requesting Withdrawals (⚡︎dB Only)

Only ⚡︎dB (Charged Decibels) are eligible for cashout.

To request a payout:

  • ✅ You must be Affiliate level or higher
  • ✅ You must hold at least 10,000 ⚡︎dB
  • ✅ You must complete KYC identity verification
  • ✅ You must pass platform review for fraud or abuse

How to request

  • Go to your Creator Dashboard
  • Navigate to: ⚡︎dB → Cashout
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your payout request

❌ dB (faded) cannot be withdrawn — they are support-only tokens that must cycle through platform activity first.

6. Security Tips

To keep your account and Decibels secure:

  • Enable device lock in the Constellation app
  • Never share your handshake codes or login device
  • Use 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) on payout-related settings
  • Log out of old devices using the Active Connections panel

If your phone is lost or stolen, contact NOIZ support immediately to revoke session access and re-authorize your account securely.

7. What Constellation Is Not

  • ❌ Not a crypto wallet
  • ❌ Not a bank or payment processor
  • ❌ Not a tool for off-platform storage or exchange
  • ❌ Not required to spend Decibels — only to manage, earn, and withdraw

Constellation is the platform’s internal identity tool, not a blockchain application or third-party finance app.

8. FAQ

Q: What if I lose my device?
A: You can revoke the device in Constellation’s Active Connections menu or contact support to regain access.

Q: Can I link other platforms to Constellation?
A: Currently, Constellation is built for NOIZ-specific services, but future integrations may be added.

Q: Can I cash out directly from Constellation?
A: No — payouts are managed via your NOIZ Creator Dashboard once all requirements are met.

Q: Why do my dB and ⚡︎dB look different?
A: dB is what you’ve purchased; ⚡︎dB is what you’ve earned. Only ⚡︎dB are eligible for payout.
