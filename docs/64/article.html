Quests are NOIZ's interactive challenge and rewards system. They replace "Drops" and milestone systems from other platforms with transparent, opt-in goals for both viewers and streamers.

1. What Are Quests on NOIZ?

Quests are structured goals, challenges, or campaigns that:

  • Reward viewers for interacting with streams
  • Reward streamers for broadcasting under specific conditions
  • Replace “Drops,” “Watch Streaks,” and “Milestones” from other platforms
  • Power sponsored campaigns, seasonal events, and grassroots promotions

Rewards may include ⚡︎dB (charged Decibels), collectible badges, marketplace items, and emotes.

2. Types of Quests

TypeDescription
📺 Viewer QuestsWatch time, chat interaction, reactions, or supporting a streamer
🧑‍💻 Streamer QuestsBroadcast a game, tag properly, maintain chat activity, hit viewer goals
🎯 Sponsored QuestsCampaigns run by verified partners, game studios, or NOIZ itself
🔁 Recurring QuestsWeekly or monthly refreshes to boost engagement or reward loyalty

3. How to Join a Quest

For Viewers:

  • Visit any stream with a Quest-enabled plugin
  • Click the “Quest Tracker” icon
  • Opt into the active campaign (if needed)
  • Progress is tracked automatically

For Streamers:

  • Visit Dashboard → Quests → Available Campaigns
  • Accept or schedule quests
  • Stream under the required conditions

4. Quest Objectives Might Include

✅ Viewer Objectives:

  • Watch a Quest stream for 60+ minutes
  • Use reactions, chat, or Vibes
  • Send ⚡︎dB or trigger milestones
  • Complete plugin interactions (polls, quizzes)

✅ Streamer Objectives:

  • Use the correct category and tags
  • Maintain chat engagement (anti-AFK)
  • Keep the Quest plugin visible
  • Reach viewer retention or boost goals

5. Rewards from Quests

  • ⚡︎dB (charged Decibels)
  • Unique badges, overlays, or cosmetics
  • Marketplace credits
  • Reverb (channel point) achievements
  • Referral tracking (for Ambassadors)

Sponsored Quests may include bonus rewards backed by NOIZ or external partners.

6. Streamer Rules for Running Quests

To maintain Quest eligibility, streamers must:

  • Moderate chat actively — no AFK streams
  • Meet the campaign’s tagging and category requirements
  • Keep Quest plugins active and visible
  • Disclose paid sponsorships (if applicable)

Violations may result in:

  • Quest being marked incomplete
  • ⚡︎dB or badge rewards being withheld
  • Temporary removal from Quest rotation

7. Quest Plugin Overview

The official Quest Plugin:

  • Displays viewer progress and goals
  • Shows percentage complete, reward previews, and interactive actions
  • Can be themed and positioned through overlay controls
  • May include polls, reaction boosts, or choice-driven progression

Streamers should test layout and visibility before going live.

8. FAQ

Can I create my own quests as a streamer?
Yes — via “Create a Quest Campaign” in your dashboard.
Can I stack multiple quests?
Sometimes — depends on the campaign rules.
Can I cheat quests with bots or muted embeds?
No — muted or inactive sessions are excluded from progress.
Do quests expire?
Yes — most have deadlines or must be completed within a live session window.
