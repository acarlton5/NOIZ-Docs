1. Why Security Matters on NOIZ

Plugins can interact with your stream, chat, overlays, and viewer data. To ensure safety:

  • All Marketplace plugins are sandboxed
  • Only verified developers can submit plugin packages
  • NOIZ enforces strict permission requests (similar to mobile apps or browser extensions)

This prevents:

  • Malicious redirects
  • Data scraping or off-platform tracking
  • UI hijacking or scam prompts
