Tour of the Creator Hub

The Creator Hub is your personal command center on NOIZ. From streaming controls to monetization tools and viewer engagement insights, everything you need to grow and manage your presence lives here. This guide provides a full walkthrough of the features available within your dashboard.

Accessing Your Dashboard

You can access the Creator Hub by navigating to the NOIZ homepage and selecting Dashboard from the top menu (visible after logging in).

Access is restricted to users who have:

  • A verified NOIZ account
  • An active Constellation-linked identity (authentication required)

As your creator journey progresses, your dashboard will unlock additional features based on your badge tier:

  • Verified: Access to stream tools, Reverb settings, and viewer quests
  • Affiliate: Monetization controls and support analytics
  • Partner & Partner+: Enhanced features, early access tools, and expanded insights

Overview Panel

The overview section is a high-level snapshot of your channel's current state and recent performance.

Displayed metrics include:

  • ⚡︎dB earned during the current platform cycle
  • Live/offline status of your stream
  • Echostage boost level and eligibility indicators
  • Active quests currently running on your channel
  • Viewer participation stats (Quest completions, Vibe triggers, Reverb usage)

Think of this as your "at-a-glance dashboard health check."

Stream Controls

This section manages how your stream looks, behaves, and interacts with the broader platform:

  • Go Live / Stop Stream (Available via NOIZ Studio or approved OBS plugin)
  • Select category and game tags from NOIZ’s discovery index
  • Apply stream ratings: Choose from Family-Friendly, Teen, or Mature
  • Latency toggle: Choose between automatic, manual, or relay override settings
  • Embed tools: Generate embeddable links or preview how your stream appears on NOIZ

Monetization Tools

Once monetization is enabled (post-KYC and Affiliate status), this panel becomes active.

Features include:

  • ⚡︎dB Redemption controls (available once you hit 10,000 ⚡︎dB)
  • Viewer support log (gifts, tips, quest completions)
  • Sub gifting and Boost tracking per stream
  • Marketplace earnings overview (for Artists and Developers)
  • Split percentage display, based on your tier (50%–80%)
  • Real-time monetization status and earnings progress

This is where creators manage their support potential — within NOIZ’s virtual support token framing.

Chat & Engagement Settings

Build, moderate, and customize your community using the following tools:

  • Reverb system customization: Set viewer reward tiers and point names
  • Viewer redemptions: Add interactive rewards that users can trigger
  • Vibe milestone progress: Track your cumulative Vibe activity (100 to 1M+)
  • Chat command editor: Add bots, cooldowns, emoji integrations
  • Mod tool access: Grant roles, trust badges, and set permissions for editors or moderators

Analytics & Insights

Understand your audience and performance with platform-side metrics. Tools include:

  • Viewer retention over time (average watch duration, return frequency)
  • Quest participation reports: Who completed which quests and when
  • Vibe heatmaps: Visualize when your stream generates the most engagement
  • Badge milestone timelines: See when users unlock 1-month, 6-month, 1-year badges

Analytics are designed to encourage healthy growth — not grind.

Marketplace Tools (For Artists or Developers)

If you've been approved as an Artist or Developer, this section appears in your dashboard.

Available tools:

  • List assets for support-based redemption: overlays, plugins, emotes, audio packs
  • Track usage: See which users have redeemed your content
  • View feedback or disputes, if any arise
  • Set tier unlocks: Gate content based on sub status, ⚡︎dB threshold, or loyalty

NOIZ doesn’t allow fiat-based unlocks — these listings are accessed via Decibel support.

Profile & Customization

Adjust your public-facing presence:

  • Upload a channel banner, profile avatar, and write your bio
  • Set badge display logic (choose whether to show your sub badge, vibe badge, etc.)
  • Check your Constellation connection status
  • Update your language, region, and discovery tags for more accurate visibility

This is where creators shape their brand on NOIZ.

Settings & Safety Tools

Manage privacy and safety from your dashboard:

  • Toggle visibility of your ⚡︎dB balance, Vibe count, and other stats
  • Set relay preferences: Auto-route, regional override, or manual relay selection
  • Report abuse or submit platform moderation concerns
  • Temporarily deactivate streaming access or review account logs
  • View active sessions and permissions via Constellation → Active Connections

Safety, transparency, and user control are baked into the NOIZ experience.
