Milestones track long-term viewer engagement across channels, quests, and support actions. They reward loyalty with cosmetic badges, achievements, and visible status in chat or your profile.

1. What Are Viewer Milestones?

Milestones are cumulative progress goals tracked per channel and across NOIZ.

They reward viewers for:

  • ⏳ Time watched
  • 💸 Sub duration or ⚡︎dB tipped
  • 🎯 Quests completed
  • 🔊 Reverb redemptions
  • ✂️ Clip contributions
  • 🔥 Hype and sticker engagement

Milestones may unlock:

  • Chat badges
  • Achievements
  • Cosmetic rewards
  • Leaderboard or clip credit

2. Types of Milestones

CategoryExamples
⏳ Time-based“Watched 10 hours,” “50 hours in one channel”
💸 Support-based“Subbed 3 months,” “Gifted 100 subs,” “Tipped 1,000 ⚡︎dB”
🎯 Quest-based“Completed 10 quests,” “3 campaign completions”
🔊 Reverb usage“Redeemed 100 channel rewards,” “Triggered 5 overlays”
✂️ Clip-related“Clipped 5 moments,” “Top clip of the week”
🔥 Engagement“Hit 5 Hype Moments,” “Spawned 100 stickers”

Most milestones are channel-specific, but some are global, tied to your viewer identity.

3. Achievement System

Achievements are digital trophies that appear in:

  • Your public profile (toggleable)
  • Your Clip credits (if featured)
  • Streamer dashboards (e.g., “Top Gifter”)

Some achievements unlock cosmetics like:

  • Emote variants
  • Chat borders
  • Profile effects

Examples include:

  • 🏆 Gift King – 1,000 gifted subs
  • 🧭 Pathfinder – 25 unique quests completed
  • 🎤 Echolord – 100+ Hype Moments triggered
  • ✨ Scene Stealer – Clip featured on Echostage

4. Milestone Badges

Certain milestones grant visible chat badges, which can appear:

  • Next to your name in chat
  • On-stream (via plugin effects)
  • In Marketplace ratings (e.g., Verified Supporter)

Badge Types:

  • Sub Duration: 1m, 2m, 3m, 6m, 1y → up to 10y
  • Gift Giver: 5, 10, 25, 50, 100 → up to 5,000
  • Time Watched: Tracked per-channel
  • Quest Champ: Based on campaign completions
  • Clip Contributor: Based on approved or featured clips

🛈 In your own chat, time-based badges are shown.
🛈 In other channels, your Affiliate/Partner tier is shown instead.

5. Where to Track Progress

To view your milestones:
Profile → Achievements & Badges

There you can:

  • Equip or rotate chat badges
  • View progress bars toward next reward
  • Filter by channel or category
  • See stats like:
  • Total ⚡︎dB given
  • Hours watched
  • Quests completed
  • Clips submitted

Streamers can also view top supporters via:
Dashboard → Channel Insights → Viewer Milestones

6. FAQ

Can I hide my milestones?
→ Yes — toggle visibility for badges and profile stats

Do milestones reset each year?
→ No — they’re cumulative unless marked as seasonal

Can I earn milestones across multiple channels?
→ Yes — most are tracked per-channel or platform-wide

Are milestones public?
→ Only when shared or equipped as badges
