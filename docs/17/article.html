How Payouts Work on NOIZ

NOIZ operates on a virtual token support system, where creators earn rewards through Charged Decibels (⚡︎dB).

Payouts occur only when:

  • ⚡︎dB thresholds are met
  • Monetization eligibility is confirmed

⚡︎dB can only be earned through:

  • Support from other users (not yourself)
  • Viewer actions like subscriptions, tips, quests, boosts, or marketplace interactions

⚠️ Faded Decibels (dB) — which are purchased tokens — cannot be redeemed for fiat.

Who Is Eligible for Payouts

You must meet all of the following criteria to redeem ⚡︎dB:

  • Be at Affiliate level or higher
  • Hold a minimum of 10,000 ⚡︎dB
  • Have completed KYC and submitted accurate payout information
  • Pass a platform review to ensure no abuse, fraud, or self-supporting behavior

⚡︎dB to Fiat Conversion Value

For eligible creators:

  • 1 ⚡︎dB = $0.01 USD

This is a fixed internal recognition rate for reward purposes — it does not represent a tradable value or token exchange rate.

dB cannot be converted into fiat. Only ⚡︎dB (earned through legitimate user support) qualify for cashout.

Revenue Splits by Creator Tier

Tier Estimated ⚡︎dB-to-Creator Split
Affiliate 50%
Partner 65%
Partner+ 75%+ (invite-only)

Example: A viewer gifts 500 dB
→ Streamer receives 250 ⚡︎dB (if Affiliate)
→ Remaining value supports platform infrastructure, relays, moderation, and quest pools

Splits are calculated based on Decibel input value, not fiat.

Payout Schedule

  • Payout requests are reviewed weekly
  • Once approved, funds are processed within 5–10 business days
  • Minimum payout = 10,000 ⚡︎dB ($100 equivalent)
  • You may choose to:
    • Hold ⚡︎dB and use them within the platform (support, marketplace, etc.)
    • Redeem them once the payout threshold is met

Where to Request a Payout

  • Go to: Creator Dashboard → ⚡︎dB → Cashout
  • If you don’t see the cashout option, it may be because:
    • You have not reached Affiliate status
    • You have not passed KYC or completed payout info
    • You hold only dB (faded), not ⚡︎dB (charged)

Your Constellation panel will show:

  • Total Decibels
  • ⚡︎dB eligible for cashout
  • Pending ⚡︎dB still under review or threshold

Important Notes

  • NOIZ does not auto-convert your ⚡︎dB — you choose when to request payout
  • ⚡︎dB may also be reused inside NOIZ (e.g., support other creators, unlock items)
  • Attempting to:
    • Loop purchased dB into ⚡︎dB
    • Gift Decibels to yourself via second accounts
    • Artificially trigger quests or boosts
    may result in payout restriction or monetization suspension

FAQ

  • Why can’t I cash out my dB?
    A: dB are support-only tokens. Only ⚡︎dB — earned through community interaction — are eligible for payout.
  • Why is my payout request pending or held?
    A: Payouts may be held for review if:
    • KYC is incomplete
    • Anti-fraud systems flag unusual patterns
    • The Decibels were not properly charged
  • Can I change my revenue split?
    A: Splits are automatically determined by your creator tier. Advancement to higher tiers increases your split rate.
  • Will NOIZ report payouts to tax authorities?
    A: Yes. All payouts are recorded, and NOIZ complies with applicable financial regulations. You may receive a tax form depending on your region and payout total.
