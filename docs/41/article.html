What Happens Next

Once a report is submitted:

  • It is reviewed by:
    • Local Mods (for light chat issues)
    • DCT (for anything platform-wide or serious)
  • The system checks:
    • Previous reports against the account
    • Related chat history, stream metadata, or linked behavior

Possible actions:

  • Warning or timeout (minor cases)
  • Account muting, suspension, or ‚ö°Ô∏édB freeze
  • Plugin or asset listing takedown
  • Escalation to Staff Admin for legal or safety concerns

What You‚Äôll See (As the Reporter)

Depending on the case:

  • You may see a silent resolution
  • You may receive a confirmation of action (if serious)

You will never receive detailed punishment information or private user data.

False or Abusive Reports

Spam or retaliatory reporting is itself a violation.

Consequences include:

  • Temporary or permanent loss of reporting tools
  • Channel moderation suspension
  • Monetization freeze (for streamers misusing mass-reporting)

Emergency or High-Risk Reports

Use the Emergency Flag if:

  • A user threatens real-world violence or self-harm
  • A minor is being exploited or targeted
  • A creator is in visible distress or danger during stream

These reports are reviewed immediately by Staff Admin and may trigger:

  • Stream takedown or lockdown
  • ‚ö°Ô∏édB and chat freeze
  • Law enforcement or local authority coordination (where legal)
