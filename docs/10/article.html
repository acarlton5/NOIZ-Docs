Earning Before Affiliate – What You Can Do

Yes — You Can Earn Before Affiliate

On NOIZ, creators are eligible for monetization as soon as they verify their identity and complete required legal documents. You don’t need to be a badge-holding Affiliate or Partner to start earning ⚡︎dB.

  • ⚡︎dB (Charged Decibels) can be earned through platform activity immediately after onboarding
  • You do not need to hit a viewer count, follower number, or stream hour minimum to unlock monetization
  • The Affiliate badge does not gate access to cashout — legal verification is the only requirement
  • NOIZ separates badges from monetization to support creators from day one, without artificial milestones

How You Can Accumulate ⚡︎dB Early

Once your legal forms are complete, you're fully eligible to earn and withdraw ⚡︎dB.

You earn ⚡︎dB when:

  • Viewers support you with Decibels (subs, boosts, tips, etc.)
  • Subs are gifted to your channel (each 500 dB sub becomes ⚡︎dB)
  • Viewers complete Quests on your channel
  • Users support your Marketplace listings (if you’re an Artist or Developer)

⚡︎dB accumulates through direct community support — it is never earned by purchasing Decibels yourself.

What the Affiliate Badge Actually Unlocks

The Affiliate badge is a recognition level — not a gate to monetization.

What it changes:

  • Revenue split increases (from Verified to Affiliate, you go from 50% to 60%)
  • Sub badge visibility in other chats (Affiliate becomes your default chat badge outside your own channel)
  • Unlocks additional emote slots, support badge tiers, and access to certain analytics

It does not unlock monetization or cashout — those begin once you're verified and complete your paperwork.

Why Monetization Starts Early

Unlike Twitch or YouTube, NOIZ doesn't wait until you're "big enough" to be rewarded.

  • ⚡︎dB earned before Affiliate can be cashed out at any time — no need to reapply or reach a tier
  • Every viewer action that supports you helps your long-term growth
  • Affiliate, Partner, and Partner+ simply enhance your tools and recognition — they don’t unlock earning
  • NOIZ believes creators should be rewarded for activity and community support — not artificial thresholds

What You Can Do With ⚡︎dB Pre-Affiliate

With even a single ⚡︎dB in your account, you already have access to:

  • Cashout tools (once you hit the 10,000 ⚡︎dB minimum)
  • Monetization dashboard (support history, top contributors)
  • Progress tracking for badge milestones
  • Participation in sponsored Quests (if available)

Your journey as a monetized creator starts the moment your legal access is cleared — badges are just recognition, not requirements.

Tips to Grow Faster (Optional)

If you're working toward Affiliate or beyond, these can help:

  • Stream consistently — the algorithm values activity over size
  • Use correct stream tags and maturity labels for visibility
  • Enable and refresh Quests often to drive engagement
  • Participate in platform-wide events and Quest chains
  • Let viewers know they can support with dB — but never pressure or guilt

Badges will come naturally as you engage and grow.

FAQ

Can I earn ⚡︎dB without the Affiliate badge?
Yes. Once verified, you are fully monetized and can cash out ⚡︎dB as long as you've reached 10,000.
What is the purpose of the Affiliate badge then?
It’s a recognition tier — it improves your rev split, unlocks cosmetic perks, and signals trust to viewers.
Do I need to apply for Affiliate?
No. NOIZ automatically awards badge tiers once you meet progression thresholds.
If I hit 10,000 ⚡︎dB before Affiliate, can I cash out?
Yes, absolutely — as long as your legal documents are complete and your account is verified.
