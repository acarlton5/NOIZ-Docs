Before You Begin

NOIZ account creation is handled through the Constellation app. Your identity is tied to your verified Constellation ID, where you assign yourself a username. You'll need a verified email and access to the Constellation app on your device.

Registering a New NOIZ Account

  1. Open the NOIZ platform and choose “Register.”
  2. You’ll be given a Connection Token.
  3. In your Constellation app, tap Keys → Register New Platform.
  4. Enter the Connection Token.
  5. Generate a 6-digit Handshake Code.
  6. Enter the 6-digit code in NOIZ to complete the secure handshake and link your identity.

Connecting to an Existing NOIZ Account

  1. If you’ve previously registered NOIZ in Constellation, open the app and go to Keys.
  2. Tap NOIZ in the list.
  3. Generate a 6-digit Handshake Code.
  4. Enter the code in NOIZ to authorize access.

How Constellation Handles Authentication

  • Replaces traditional 2FA, password, and username fields.
  • Authentication is encrypted and device-linked via Constellation.
  • All platform access is managed through your verified Constellation identity.
  • You can revoke access or manage sessions from Constellation → Keys → Active Connections.

Verifying Your Identity (for Monetization & Access Unlocks)

Why we ask for identity verification:

  • Prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Unlock monetization (⚡︎dB redemptions, quests, marketplace, etc.).

What we ask for:

  • Valid government-issued ID.
  • Legal name (not shown publicly).

What verification enables:

  • Monetization features.
  • Marketplace listing.
  • Creator badges and visibility.
