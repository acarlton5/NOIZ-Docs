Developer Monetization & Usage Tiers

1. How Developers Earn on NOIZ

NOIZ rewards developers with ⚡︎Decibels (charged) when they:

  • Complete Dev Quests
  • List plugins or interactive tools in the Marketplace
  • Offer custom overlays or utilities through approved channels
  • Collaborate with streamers on features used in Partner+ content

⚡︎dB earned this way can be:

  • Used to unlock other content or support creators
  • Eligible for cashout (if charged and thresholds are met)
  • Counted toward badge progression and tier unlocks

⚠️ All earnings must come from on-platform actions — no crypto, fiat, or third-party payment systems are allowed.

3. Monetization Options

Developers can earn ⚡︎dB by:

  • Setting a ⚡︎dB unlock on their plugin in the Marketplace
  • Completing Dev Quests assigned by NOIZ
  • Being selected for seasonal Quest Packs or sponsored overlay packs
  • Receiving direct support via streamer collaborations (e.g., co-built rewards)

Developers may not:

  • Offer tools for off-platform payment
  • Solicit external donations for plugin access
  • Include cash pricing or external payment links in descriptions

5. Progression & Maintenance

To keep Developer perks active:

  • Maintain at least one up-to-date plugin/tool every 90 days
  • Respond to moderation requests within 5 days
  • Fix flagged issues or bugs promptly
  • Remain in good standing with the Plugin SDK Terms of Use

Inactive or non-compliant developers may have their badge temporarily removed.

6. Developer Collaboration

Developers can:

  • Partner with Artists to bundle plugins and overlays
  • Create tools for individual streamers (with their consent)
  • Join NOIZ-led Quest Campaigns (if invited)

Developers cannot:

  • Impersonate NOIZ staff
  • Claim official status or exclusivity without approval
  • Release “private” tools without going through plugin moderation

7. FAQ

Can I monetize plugins outside NOIZ? → No — monetization must be via ⚡︎dB inside NOIZ.

Can I promote my plugin on social media? → Yes — keep all pricing/CTA language within platform-safe terms.

Do Dev Quests pay cash? → ⚡︎dB rewards may be eligible for cashout if charged and thresholds are met.

Can I bundle overlays and plugins? → Yes — both must pass their respective submission reviews.

How do I upgrade tiers? → NOIZ staff review developer contributions regularly and issue tier updates.
