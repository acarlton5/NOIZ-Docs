What This Policy Covers

This document outlines how Decibels (dB) and Charged Decibels (⚡︎dB) function within the NOIZ platform—including how they are stored, viewed, and transferred between users.

It also clarifies that:

  • Constellation acts as an access ledger, not a wallet, exchange, or banking service.
  • dB and ⚡︎dB are digital support tokens—never legal tender or financial instruments.
  • Redemption rules apply only to ⚡︎dB, and only if earned through community activity.

This policy is designed to protect NOIZ’s legal compliance, discourage abuse, and ensure that our reward systems are fair, non-speculative, and platform-contained.

What Is Constellation?

Constellation is the access and identity system used across the NOIZ ecosystem. In addition to authentication, it acts as your virtual token container, tracking and displaying:

  • Your dB balance (Decibels you’ve unlocked to support creators)
  • Your ⚡︎dB balance (Charged Decibels you’ve earned from others)
  • Your transaction history (sent, received, or earned)
  • Your connected apps and services

Importantly, Constellation is not a financial wallet. It cannot:

  • Hold fiat funds
  • Facilitate withdrawals beyond what NOIZ explicitly allows
  • Interact with external marketplaces or cryptocurrencies

It’s a closed system, similar to digital credits in a game or tokens on a loyalty platform.

How dB and ⚡︎dB Work

Type Description Exchangeable? Redemption Eligible?
dB Unlocked through Constellation ✅ Yes ❌ No
⚡︎dB Earned via support actions on NOIZ ✅ Yes ✅ Yes (if eligible)

dB are faded Decibels—they’re acquired by unlocking support tokens in Constellation and can only be used to interact within the platform. Once dB are used to support another user (e.g., through tipping, gifting subs, or marketplace actions), they become ⚡︎dB for that user.

This system ensures that only earned support, not unlocked tokens, become eligible for redemption.

dB Usage Rules

Your dB balance can be used for digital support actions such as:

  • Tipping creators
  • Gifting subscriptions
  • Boosting streams
  • Unlocking marketplace items (e.g., emotes, overlays, plugins)

However:

  • Once used, dB cannot be refunded (unless a verified system error occurs)
  • dB cannot be redeemed under any circumstance
  • dB are not legal tender, and cannot be used to obtain physical or off-platform goods

Think of dB as support tokens—they unlock digital participation and engagement, but hold no financial entitlement or exchange value.

⚡︎dB Eligibility & Access

Charged Decibels (⚡︎dB) can only be earned, never bought.

To earn ⚡︎dB, you must receive support from others via:

  • Gifted subs
  • Tips and boosts
  • Quest completions
  • Marketplace redemptions

To redeem ⚡︎dB, you must meet all of the following:

  1. Reach Affiliate status or higher
  2. Complete legal identity verification (KYC)
  3. Hold at least 10,000 ⚡︎dB in your account

You may also use ⚡︎dB in-platform for the same actions as dB (e.g., tipping other creators), or save it for redemption once eligible.

Internal-Only Exchange Model

NOIZ is a closed economy—Decibel transactions and balances are strictly limited to the NOIZ platform and Constellation ecosystem.

NOIZ does not support:

  • Crypto-asset transactions or tokens
  • Fiat withdrawals outside of approved ⚡︎dB redemption
  • Third-party trading, resale, or swaps
  • NFTs or token speculation

All activity is internally monitored and legally firewalled to prevent misuse or misrepresentation of Decibels as financial assets.

Misuse & Account Flagging

Attempting to exploit the Decibel system—particularly by converting dB into ⚡︎dB through artificial or self-driven actions—is prohibited.

Examples of prohibited behavior include:

  • Tipping yourself using alternate accounts
  • Looping dB through shell accounts
  • Attempting to bypass KYC verification for redemption

If abuse is detected, NOIZ may:

  • Flag your account for review
  • Remove ⚡︎dB that was gained improperly
  • Restrict monetization access or freeze redemption privileges

Our priority is to keep the system safe for creators who participate fairly.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I convert my dB to ⚡︎dB directly?
No. dB becomes ⚡︎dB only when another user supports you with them.
Can I send ⚡︎dB to another creator?
Yes—⚡︎dB can be used just like dB for support actions. However, ⚡︎dB received from others still counts toward your redeemable balance.
What if I accidentally support the wrong user?
Support actions are final. You may open a support ticket for review if a verified mistake or exploit occurred.
Can I use Decibels on other platforms or tools?
No. Decibels are exclusive to NOIZ and Constellation. They are not portable or usable elsewhere.
