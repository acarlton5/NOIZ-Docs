NOIZ allows creators to simulcast their streams to other platforms — as long as core engagement, visibility, and plugin systems remain respected.

1. What Is Dual Streaming?

Dual streaming (or simulcasting) means broadcasting the same live content on NOIZ and other platforms at the same time — such as:

  • Twitch
  • YouTube
  • Kick
  • Facebook Live

This guide outlines how dual streaming works within NOIZ’s creator rules, and what actions may violate campaign or moderation policies.

2. Is Dual Streaming Allowed on NOIZ?

✅ Yes — NOIZ supports simulcasting by default.

We believe creators should:

  • Grow across multiple platforms
  • Reach broader audiences
  • Retain ownership of their content and discoverability

There are no exclusivity clauses required to stream on NOIZ.

3. What’s Not Allowed

❌ Dual streaming becomes a violation if you:

  • Advertise other platforms mid-stream
    • Example: “Come to Twitch for drops” or “Going live on YouTube now instead”
  • Use NOIZ as a redirect tool only
    • i.e., passive camera with no engagement, zero chat presence, or clear intent to bounce users elsewhere
  • Neglect NOIZ chat or viewers during streams — especially if:
    • You’re running Quests that require viewer interaction
    • You ignore Quest overlay events, plugin triggers, or Decibel support
  • Break Quest campaign terms
    • If a Sponsored Quest specifies “NOIZ-first” or “exclusive stream window,” you must follow those terms
  • Auto-redirect NOIZ users through overlays, commands, or pinned links

4. Acceptable Use Cases

✅ Dual streaming is fully permitted when:

  • You actively participate in both chats, or have mods supporting each
  • Your content is the same across platforms — not favoring one with perks or interactivity
  • You don’t give extra benefits (drops, unlocks, giveaways) to non-NOIZ platforms
  • You don’t embed other chat overlays that drown out NOIZ visuals or plugin visibility

If your viewers feel like the NOIZ experience is “lesser” than other platforms, you may be asked to adjust.

5. Tool Support

NOIZ supports all major dual-streaming setups:

  • 🧩 NOIZ Studio + third-party restream services (e.g., Restream.io, Castr)
  • 🎥 OBS Studio with multi-RTMP plugin
  • 🛰️ Pathweaver relays that forward streams from custom nodes
  • ⚙️ Other tools that treat NOIZ as an equal destination — not a backup or mirror

Avoid setups that down-prioritize NOIZ performance or overlay accuracy.

6. Important Notes for Sponsored Quests

If you're participating in a Sponsored Quest or verified Partner Campaign, always check the terms.

🚫 If the campaign includes an exclusivity clause or “NOIZ-first” rule, you must:

  • Prioritize NOIZ chat and viewer interaction
  • Avoid linking or promoting your other stream locations during the quest
  • Keep your Quest overlays, plugins, or triggers active and visible
  • Maintain stream focus on the NOIZ experience

Violation of these rules can result in:

  • ⚠️ Quest progress reset
  • ❌ ⚡︎dB being withheld
  • 🚫 Future campaign access removed

7. FAQ

Can I simulcast if I’m not in a campaign?
→ Yes, as long as you follow platform behavior standards.

Can I run a sub or view goal tied to Twitch?
→ No. Don’t display goals, incentives, or pinned messages that are exclusive to other platforms during a NOIZ stream.

Can I send people to my YouTube VODs or Twitch clips?
→ Yes — off-stream linking is fine. But don’t tell viewers to leave NOIZ for another live broadcast.

Can I embed my Twitch chat on stream?
→ Not recommended. It creates priority conflict and may interfere with NOIZ’s moderation and plugin overlays.
