Plugins on NOIZ transform your stream into an interactive experience by adding modular tools, overlays, and viewer-responsive systems. They're designed to be lightweight, customizable, and safe.

1. What Are Plugins on NOIZ?

Plugins are interactive modules that streamers can add to their channel or stream layout. They enhance engagement, enable real-time viewer interaction, and provide new tools for customizing stream behavior.

Plugins can appear as:

  • On-screen overlays (Quests, Reactions, Polls)
  • Chat-integrated bots, shoutouts, or mini-games
  • Viewer-side HUDs for progress tracking or goals
  • Developer-created tools from the Plugin Marketplace

3. How to Enable or Manage Plugins

Go to Dashboard ‚Üí Plugins ‚Üí My Installed. Here, you can:

  • Browse and install verified plugin packs from the Marketplace
  • Enable or disable plugins per scene or stream
  • Set plugin placement and layer priority using drag-and-drop tools
  • Assign visibility conditions (e.g., follower-only, supporter-only)

Many plugins also support:

  • Chat command triggers (e.g. !poll or !vibe)
  • Hotkey/Stream Deck integration
  • Cooldown timers to prevent spam

4. How Viewers Use Plugins

As a viewer, you may see and interact with:

  • Quest trackers
  • Live polls or trivia
  • Click-to-boost animations and Hype triggers
  • Emote bursts, Vibe triggers, or reaction meters

All viewer-facing plugins:

  • Respect age/maturity settings
  • Can be disabled in Viewer Settings ‚Üí Interactivity
  • Will always request consent before displaying persistent UI

5. Plugin Trust & Safety

Verified plugins:

  • Are reviewed by the NOIZ Dev Team for compliance and performance
  • Cannot access personal or private data
  • Are sandboxed to prevent redirect exploits or external site abuse

  • Plugins listed in the official NOIZ Marketplace
  • Developer-signed .npk plugin bundles (for advanced users)

6. Plugin Settings and Customization

Most plugins support:

  • Theming (light/dark modes, transparency levels)
  • Positioning (HUD corner, inline, fullscreen layers)
  • Trigger visibility (on sub, hype moment, or Quest event)
  • Stream-specific behavior (different overlays per scene)

Plugins can also be layered or chained:

  • Sub Train ‚Üí Emote Explosion ‚Üí Vibe Multiplier
  • Quest Completion ‚Üí Animated Unlock ‚Üí Auto-Shoutout

These behaviors can be managed in your Plugin Configuration Panel under your stream scene tools.
