Who Are Pathweavers?

Pathweavers are trusted community members who run NOIZ relay nodes — specialized server instances that help:

  • Distribute streaming bandwidth
  • Reduce latency for regional viewers
  • Strengthen network resilience
  • Offset platform infrastructure costs

They are not miners or crypto validators — Pathweavers are rewarded for technical contribution, not speculation or computation.

Why the Name "Pathweaver"?

Streaming on NOIZ relies on custom routing — streams must find the lowest-latency, most reliable path to viewers.

Pathweavers “weave the route” for content by providing pathways between:

  • Streamers
  • Viewers
  • Edge servers

Their work helps shape the technical fabric of NOIZ.

What Pathweavers Do

  • ✅ Host relay nodes using provided configurations
  • ✅ Maintain uptime and connectivity logs
  • ✅ Ensure privacy and compliance with NOIZ standards
  • ✅ Follow technical upgrade schedules during rollouts
  • ✅ Respect viewer trust and data integrity

They are never exposed to:

  • Viewer account data
  • Chat messages
  • Personal identity info

All content is encrypted in transport — nodes simply relay video/audio streams between endpoints.

Pathweaver Badge

Approved node operators receive the Pathweaver badge, shown:

  • In chat
  • On user profiles
  • On the public node map (optional anonymity)

It signifies major infrastructure contribution and is distinct from roles like Developer or Streamer.

Why Host a Relay Node?

Benefits include:

  • ⚡︎dB support bonuses from the platform’s internal support budget
  • Priority in early test rollouts of infrastructure features
  • Eligibility for Pathweaver milestones
  • Recognition as a foundational member of NOIZ’s architecture

Relay nodes draw from a fixed platform reserve — they do not “mint” or generate new value like crypto systems.

FAQ

  • Can anyone become a Pathweaver? → Yes — if you meet the requirements and pass screening
  • Do I earn money? → You earn ⚡︎dB support, which may be eligible for cashout under platform policy
  • Can I resell access to my node? → No — unauthorized traffic or node monetization is forbidden
  • Can I lose Pathweaver status? → Yes — for downtime, abuse, or policy violations
  • Is this crypto or mining? → No — this is stream routing, not computation or token minting
