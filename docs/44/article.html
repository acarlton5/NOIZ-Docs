Chat Filters, Block Lists, and Safety Controls

NOIZ empowers both creators and viewers to control their experience. These tools are designed to support a welcoming, non-hostile environment without requiring full bans or platform-wide action.

AutoMod & Keyword Filters

  • Flags or hides messages containing offensive, sexual, or disruptive language
  • Filters messages before they appear in chat
  • Supports tiered severity settings:
LevelDescription
1Light filtering (slurs, threats)
2Moderate filtering (innuendo, aggression)
3+Strict (caps, spam, emotional triggers)

Custom Blocked Words List

  • Streamers can block specific words or phrases
  • Mods can request additions live in chat
  • Wildcards supported (e.g., *kill*, d*mn, etc.)

User-Level Chat Blocking

  • Block specific users from interacting or pinging them
  • Hide messages globally from blocked users (only for the blocker)
  • Prevent mentions, whispers, and message highlighting

Streamers may also:

  • Block users from interacting with their chat entirely
  • Restrict specific users from using:
    • Emotes
    • Overlay reactions
    • Plugins and boosts

Message Highlighting & Mod Tools

  • Highlight flagged messages for visibility or moderation review
  • Use “Flag for DCT Review” to send messages directly to platform enforcement
  • Trigger Timed Slowmode or cooldowns during high-traffic or heated conversations

Channel Safety Controls

Streamers can enable:

  • Chat Delay for new or unverified users
  • Hold-to-View gates for sensitive streams
  • Chat access restrictions including:
    • Verified Constellation account
    • Linked game account (for Quest-specific access)
    • Follower-only or Supporter-only modes

These controls help reduce spam, harassment, and automated attacks.

Whisper & DM Restrictions

Whispers are:

  • Defaulted to followers or known allies
  • Only available after mutual interaction or shared quest history
  • Blockable via stream or viewer privacy settings

Streamers can:

  • Disable all whispers
  • Prevent DM link sharing for safety or spam prevention

Advanced Filtering Options (via NOIZ Labs)

Optional tools for deeper chat control:

  • Sentiment detection: Flag sarcasm, hostility, or baiting
  • Reaction moderation: Block or filter animated reactions/emotes
  • Auto-responses: Bots respond to filtered terms with community-safe messages (e.g., “Let’s keep it respectful here.”)

These tools are available through approved Marketplace plugins and do not require external software.

FAQ

Can I block someone from seeing my stream?
Only through Mature tagging, regional restrictions, or Quest gating.

Can block lists carry across streams?
Yes. Viewer-level blocks are saved in Constellation and persist across NOIZ.

What happens to messages from blocked users?
They’re hidden only for the person who blocked them — unless they are flagged or AutoMod catches them.
