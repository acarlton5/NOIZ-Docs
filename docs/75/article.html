Sponsored Quest Campaigns are co-developed events between NOIZ and verified partners to launch games, showcase Marketplace creators, or run seasonal promotions.

They combine viewer and streamer objectives with ⚡︎dB, cosmetic, or badge rewards — never cash or physical prizes unless handled directly by NOIZ.

1. What Is a Sponsored Quest Campaign?

A Sponsored Quest Campaign is a branded activation that includes:

  • Time-limited viewer & streamer Quests
  • Platform-issued rewards (⚡︎dB, emotes, overlays, badges)
  • In-stream plugin visibility and progress tracking
  • Optional Echostage or Marketplace tie-ins

These campaigns are only initiated by NOIZ staff in partnership with approved sponsors, brands, or Marketplace creators.

2. Who Can Launch a Sponsored Campaign?

Eligible groups:

  • Verified game developers or publishers
  • Companies running official NOIZ-branded integrations
  • Marketplace creators launching asset lines (via collaboration)
  • Internal NOIZ teams launching platform-wide initiatives

Not eligible:
Individual streamers or community members without verified sponsor status.
Streamers may be featured in a campaign but cannot create one independently.

3. How Collaboration Works

Campaigns are built in partnership:

  • NOIZ handles: Quest structure, reward balancing, plugin setup, moderation & reporting
  • Sponsor provides: Budget (if applicable), branding assets, event creative, and campaign-specific content
  • Streamers: May be invited to participate but do not control objectives or reward logic

All campaigns include:

  • A viewer-facing Quest tracker
  • At least one interactive stream element (plugin, reaction, clip trigger, sub milestone)
  • Reward fulfillment through ⚡︎dB or other digital-only assets

4. Sponsorship Expectations & Rules

Sponsors must:

  • Follow digital-only reward policies (no crypto, NFTs, or off-platform goods unless handled directly by NOIZ)
  • Avoid financial, gambling, or unapproved third-party promotions
  • Submit all assets, branding, and event copy for NOIZ review before launch
  • Allow moderation tools and streamer-level content control

NOIZ reserves the right to:

  • Deny, cancel, or reschedule campaigns
  • Modify Quest objectives for fairness or technical reasons
  • Disqualify abusive or low-engagement activations

5. Campaign Workflow

  1. Proposal: Sponsor submits a campaign request via NOIZ BizDev
  2. Review: NOIZ staff vet the content, assets, and objectives for compliance
  3. Build: NOIZ configures the Quest backend, plugin triggers, and reward logic
  4. Test: QA phase and invited streamer onboarding
  5. Launch: Campaign goes live with coordinated announcements
  6. Fulfillment: ⚡︎dB and rewards issued automatically via NOIZ systems
  7. Report: Sponsor receives post-campaign metrics (views, completions, engagement data)

6. FAQ

Can a streamer request to run their own campaign?
No. Streamers may be featured but cannot design or control a campaign.
Can sponsors offer keys or codes?
Yes — if NOIZ handles all distribution internally.
Can a sponsor promote a campaign off-platform?
Yes — but all external marketing must be pre-approved by NOIZ.
Can viewers earn real money from a sponsored Quest?
No — rewards are digital, cosmetic, or ⚡︎dB only.
