NOIZ’s Core Philosophy: No Ads, No Interruptions

NOIZ does not run:

  • Pre-rolls or mid-roll ads
  • Banner placements or clickable pop-ups
  • Audio or video ads injected by third-party networks

We believe that creator spaces should belong to the creator, not be hijacked by outside interests. On NOIZ, viewer attention is treated as a relationship, not an opportunity to sell impressions.

Your stream is not an ad break — it's your space.

How Creators Earn Without Ads

NOIZ empowers creators through a support-based rewards system, not advertiser payouts.

Ways creators earn:

  • Subscriptions — 500 dB (viewer-funded virtual support)
  • Tips and boosts — sent using dB and ⚡︎dB
  • Quest participation — creators may receive ⚡︎dB for viewer completions
  • Sponsored campaign quests — opt-in, brand-backed quests that reward viewers and creators
  • No creator needs to hit view count targets or serve ads to unlock rewards.

Sponsored Quests = Our Version of “Brand Deals”

NOIZ uses sponsored quests as an ethical, engagement-first way to integrate brand content.

What they are:

  • Optional Quests backed by brands or games
  • Viewers complete goals (e.g., "watch 15 minutes of a tagged stream")
  • Rewards include:
    • Cosmetic badges
    • dB
    • Stream-specific effects or unlockables

All sponsored quests are:

  • Approved and reviewed by NOIZ staff
  • Clearly labeled with “Sponsored” tags
  • Built into the platform — no external signups or requirements

These quests replace ad banners with something interactive and rewarding.

Why We Rejected Ads Entirely

Here’s why we intentionally chose to exclude ads:

Problem With Ad-Based PlatformsNOIZ’s Response
Viewers forced to watch irrelevant adsNo pre-rolls, no interruptions
Advertisers set the rulesCreators retain control
Metrics focused on time watched, not interactionBoosts, quests, and chat engagement drive visibility
Cookie tracking and third-party surveillanceNOIZ does not use ad-tracking or sell data

NOIZ was built for creators — not for squeezing revenue from passive watch time.

How Sponsored Content Stays Ethical

We don’t just allow sponsored quests — we hold them to clear standards:

  • All sponsored content must be tagged in-stream and in Quest UI
  • Rewards are always platform-native (dB, ⚡︎dB, badges — not fiat or off-site perks)
  • No misleading incentives or forced shoutouts
  • There is no pay-to-feature path — Echostage and visibility are earned through boosts and participation
  • Sponsored quests should feel fun, not forced.

How This Benefits Viewers

For the audience, this means:

  • Immediate stream access — no waiting rooms or unskippable ads
  • No trackers, cookies, or off-platform popups
  • Viewer support goes directly to creators, not intermediaries
  • Interactive opportunities — quests, badges, boosts — replace passive viewing

The result? A cleaner, more meaningful experience for everyone.

FAQ

Q: Will NOIZ ever add ads?
A: No. NOIZ was built from the ground up to operate without them. Sponsored quests and boosts are our preferred support model.

Q: Can creators add their own off-platform sponsors?
A: Yes, but disclosures are required. You must follow our Paid Promotions Policy and clearly label sponsored content.

Q: How do I sponsor a quest as a brand?
A: Contact our Partnerships Team through the Sponsor Portal. We’ll work with you to create an ethical, action-based Quest campaign.

Q: What’s the difference between a sponsored quest and a paid promotion?
A: Sponsored quests are platform-run campaigns. Paid promotions are individual creator deals and must follow proper disclosure rules.
