KYC / Legal Policy for Monetization Access

1. Why KYC Is Required

To maintain legal compliance and enable safe, secure payouts, NOIZ requires certain users to complete KYC (Know Your Customer) verification.

This process is required to:

  • Confirm your identity, location, and age
  • Ensure you’re eligible to receive ⚡︎dB-based rewards
  • Comply with global financial regulations

⚠️ KYC is only required for creators requesting cashout access — not for casual viewers, early-stage streamers, or non-monetized users.

2. Who Needs to Complete KYC

You must complete KYC if you:

  • Have reached Affiliate status or higher
  • Hold at least 10,000 ⚡︎dB
  • Want to submit a cashout request

You may still:

  • Stream freely
  • Accumulate ⚡︎dB
  • Participate in events, quests, and receive support

…but until you’re verified, your ⚡︎dB cannot be cashed out.

3. What KYC Collects

Our licensed payout provider securely collects:

  • Legal name and age
  • Country of residence
  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Optional: Business or tax ID (for advanced tiers or region-specific compliance)

❗️NOIZ does not store your ID. All sensitive information is handled exclusively by our regulated third-party partner.

4. Where to Complete KYC

KYC is completed directly inside the Constellation app:

  1. Open your Creator Dashboard
  2. Go to: ⚡︎dB → Payout
  3. Follow the secure KYC link to upload your documents
  4. Most approvals are completed within 3–5 business days

You’ll receive a notification once you are verified.

5. How Your Data Is Used

Your personal data is used only for payout eligibility and identity verification.

We do not:

  • Sell your data
  • Share it with marketers
  • Use it for advertising

Your data is:

  • Encrypted at rest
  • Transmitted over secure TLS protocols
  • Stored only by the licensed verification and payout provider
  • Never shown to other users, mods, or staff

6. Legal Framing of Monetization

NOIZ follows a support-based recognition model, not employment or contracting.

This means:

  • ⚡︎dB = platform-issued virtual tokens
  • ⚡︎dB are rewards, not income, salary, or wages
  • Creators are not employees
  • There is no work contract or service agreement

This keeps NOIZ compliant with:

  • Gig economy laws
  • Virtual goods regulations
  • International payout standards

7. What Happens Without KYC

You can still:

  • Broadcast and grow your channel
  • Earn ⚡︎dB from viewer support
  • Participate in sponsored quests and campaigns

But you cannot:

  • Use the cashout menu
  • Submit payout requests
  • Access Partner+-level tools or marketplace listing upgrades

Your ⚡︎dB will remain in your account until you complete KYC. They are not lost or removed — just held.

8. FAQ

Is KYC required just to stream?
No. KYC is only required if you want to cash out ⚡︎dB.
What if I fail identity verification?
You will be notified and allowed to re-submit with corrected or clearer documents.
Can I re-submit if rejected?
Yes. Simply return to the same panel in Constellation and follow the instructions to re-upload your ID.
Does KYC make me a contractor or employee?
No. Completing KYC does not create any employment relationship. You remain an independent user earning virtual rewards.
