Resonance Stickers are chat-activated effects that let you show off your support in style. They range from simple chat animations to full-screen stream reactions — and they always match the Decibels you choose to spend.

💬 What Are Resonance Stickers?

Resonance Stickers are special visual (and sometimes audio) effects that appear in chat or across the stream when you support a creator using Decibels.

Think of them like themed reactions — a splash of flair that happens when you send dB.

💸 How Do They Work?

Each sticker is linked to a specific Decibel amount. When you use one:

  • It activates the effect for everyone watching
  • The streamer receives support based on the Decibel amount you chose
  • The sticker effect is tied to that support — it doesn’t change the amount

Example:

  • Water Splash costs 50 dB → light splash in chat
  • Fireball Burst costs 500 dB → animated fireball on stream + chat impact
  • You can’t adjust the price — each sticker is pre-set by its design level

🎁 How Do I Get More Stickers?

You start with a default set — but you can unlock more by:

  • Opening Echo Capsules (random cosmetic unlocks)
  • Completing certain platform quests or achievements
  • Participating in seasonal or sponsored events

Once you unlock a sticker:

  • It’s added to your collection
  • You can select it when choosing a support tier
  • It stays tied to your account

📦 What Are Echo Capsules?

Echo Capsules are NOIZ’s version of cosmetic reward packs. They may contain:

  • Emotes
  • Avatar decorations
  • Chat effects
  • Resonance Stickers

If a capsule grants you a sticker, it means you now own that effect — you still spend Decibels to trigger it.

🔁 Can I Trade or Sell Stickers?

No — stickers are yours once unlocked and can’t be:

  • Traded
  • Gifted
  • Sold
  • Converted to Decibels

They’re designed to be personal cosmetic upgrades — not currency.

📚 How Do I Use a Sticker on Stream?

  1. Click the support icon under the stream
  2. Choose the Decibel amount you want to send
  3. Pick an unlocked sticker from your collection
  4. Confirm — and your sticker blasts off into chat or the stream!

You can switch stickers at any time — or just send plain support with no sticker if you prefer.
