1. Constellation = Multi-Factor by Default

NOIZ does not use passwords or traditional login methods. Instead, all user access is secured through Constellation, our token-based access platform. This system combines multiple factors by default, including:

  • Constellation handshake codes — short-lived, random connection tokens
  • Device-based linking — your login is cryptographically tied to your Constellation device
  • Session management controls — you can revoke or pause access at any time

This model is more secure than typical 2FA:

  • ✅ No SMS-based codes (immune to SIM swapping)
  • ✅ No 3rd-party authenticator apps (nothing to install or sync)
  • ✅ No email-based recovery (prevents phishing resets)

Your device becomes your key — and you hold complete control over what gets unlocked.

2. How It Works

Every login to NOIZ follows a multi-factor, token-based handshake:

  1. You request access via NOIZ
  2. NOIZ displays a temporary connection token
  3. In your Constellation app, you either:
    • Approve the connection directly, or
    • Paste in the token and generate a new 6-digit handshake code
  4. You enter the code on NOIZ, completing the cryptographic handshake

This process verifies:

  • Your identity
  • Your device trust
  • Your session validity for accessing sensitive features (like payouts, plugins, or stream management)

There are no saved passwords, usernames, or login credentials.

3. Session Management

From the Constellation app, you can:

  • View all active sessions linked to your account (e.g. NOIZ, dashboards, plugins)
  • Revoke access instantly from any device or session
  • Lock or pause linking if you suspect your account is at risk

All session activity is tracked in a way that prioritizes privacy and control — only you can see your connected apps, and revocation happens in real-time.

4. What Replaces "Wallet Security"

Although NOIZ uses virtual tokens (dB and ⚡︎dB), it does not use wallets, crypto accounts, or asset custody.

  • Your balances are tracked on the platform, not held by your device
  • Your Constellation session is the access gate, not a holder of funds
  • There is no private key, seed phrase, or crypto wallet needed or provided

This prevents accidental loss of access and avoids the legal risks of custodial or financial framing.

5. Security Tips

To keep your account secure:

  • Enable biometric or PIN lock on your Constellation app
  • Audit your active sessions regularly
  • Never share:
    • Your 6-digit handshake code
    • A connection token generated by NOIZ
  • Report any unexpected access or ⚡︎dB changes immediately via:

Settings → Report → Security Concern

This system is designed to prevent phishing, hijacking, impersonation, and relay spoofing — even if someone has your channel name or device ID.

6. FAQ

What if I lose access to Constellation?
Log in from a new device and revoke the previous one using your backup method. If you’re completely locked out, contact NOIZ Support for recovery verification.

Can I link a backup device?
Yes. You can authorize multiple Constellation installs (e.g. phone + tablet) from within the app’s “Active Devices” menu.

Do I need an authenticator app or password manager?
No. All identity management happens within Constellation — no external tools required.

Is this like a crypto wallet?
No. Constellation is not a wallet and does not store or transfer crypto. It functions as a secure platform keychain, managing access to digital tools and platform-issued virtual tokens.
