IRL (In Real Life) streaming refers to any broadcast that takes place outside a controlled or private environment. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Parks, sidewalks, streets, beaches, or nature trails
  • Restaurants, malls, stores, or public transit areas
  • Conventions, concerts, or festivals
