Policy outlining ratings, reviews, dispute processes, and protections for the NOIZ Marketplace.

1. Why Ratings and Reviews Matter

  • Viewers identify high-quality overlays, emotes, and audio packs
  • Artists build trust and reputation
  • The platform curates featured or recommended listings
  • Flag content that doesn’t meet expectations or violates guidelines

This system supports a community-driven quality layer that complements moderation.

2. How Ratings Work

  • Viewers who unlock an asset (via ⚡︎dB support) can leave a 1–5 star rating
  • Only users who have unlocked the asset can rate it
  • Anonymous by default, but optional comments can be attached
  • Artists cannot remove reviews but may respond to them once per listing
  • ⭐ Star averages are calculated per asset and per artist storefront (globally)

3. Leaving a Review

After unlocking an asset:

  1. Visit: Profile → My Inventory → Leave Feedback
  2. Choose star rating
  3. (Optional) Add a short written comment
  4. Submit — feedback is reviewed for maturity, spam, or abuse

Reviews can be updated once if your experience with the asset changes (e.g., the artist publishes a major update).

4. What Artists Can Do

  • Respond publicly to reviews
  • Update their listing to address concerns
  • Flag abusive or fake reviews for removal
  • Ask NOIZ staff to moderate review threads if disputes escalate

⚠️ Artists cannot mass-delete reviews or selectively hide negative ones.

5. Disputes & Resolution

If you unlock an asset that:

  • Doesn’t match the description or preview
  • Is broken, low quality, or misrepresented
  • Was uploaded without proper rights

You may:

  • Report the asset
  • Flag it for “quality or license concern”
  • Request a ⚡︎dB adjustment (in rare cases, reviewed by staff)

All reports are reviewed by Marketplace Moderation staff or escalated to Staff Admins if there’s a licensing/legal implication.

6. Artist Protections

If an artist is:

  • Review-bombed
  • Targeted for platform-wide harassment
  • Blamed for plugin issues they didn’t cause

NOIZ may:

  • Filter mass reviews
  • Limit comment spam
  • Mute specific reviewers
  • Place assets in review-only mode until verified

7. FAQ

Can I leave a review if I unlocked a free asset?
Yes — reviews aren’t tied to the ⚡︎dB amount
Can I remove my own review?
You can edit it, not fully remove
Can artists ask for 5 stars?
Yes — but cannot demand or trade incentives for positive reviews
Can I request a ⚡︎dB adjustment if I dislike the asset?
No — all support-based unlocks are final, but you can report quality issues
