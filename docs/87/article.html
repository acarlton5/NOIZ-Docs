Detection & Auto-Blacklisting

NOIZ automatically flags nodes for:

  • Failing multiple relay integrity checks
  • Excessive packet loss or corrupted stream segments
  • Mismatched or expired authentication tokens
  • Rapid, unexplained region-switching

If a node triggers 3+ consecutive violations, it may be automatically blacklisted without manual review.

Permanent Exclusion

Nodes may be permanently blocked if they are:

  • Found intentionally hijacking or redirecting viewer sessions
  • Used to advertise scams, crypto services, or gambling
  • In violation of NOIZ data integrity standards (logging IPs, packet inspection)
  • Linked to repeated alt-account abuse

Permanent blacklists are applied per-node fingerprint, not just IP address.
