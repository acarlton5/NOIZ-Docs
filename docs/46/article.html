This guide protects you from impersonators, scams, and unsafe communication during account actions, plugin issues, or moderation escalations. If someone claims to be staff but violates these rules — report it immediately.

Why Safe Contact Matters

NOIZ staff will never ask you to:

  • Share your password or handshake codes
  • Send ⚡︎dB or Decibels
  • Click unverified links in DMs
  • Install external apps or tools

Scammers may pretend to be staff — especially during:

  • Account appeals
  • Plugin or Marketplace issues
  • DCT investigations or suspensions

How to Identify Official NOIZ Staff

✅ All real staff will have verified platform-assigned badges, such as:

  • 🛡️ DCT – Enforcement & global moderation
  • 🧠 Staff Admin – Legal, safety, and escalation team
  • 👨‍💻 Staff Developer – Internal plugin/game/infrastructure support
  • 🎤 Partner Relations – Outreach & campaign liaisons

Staff will only contact you via:

  • The in-app NOIZ inbox
  • Verified @noiz.gg email domains
  • Platform-based Constellation notifications (visible in your session history)

❗ NOIZ staff will not contact you via Discord, Twitter, Telegram, or personal social apps unless previously arranged through NOIZ itself.

How to Contact NOIZ Staff (Safely)

🧾 Approved support paths:

  • Appeals or moderation issues: → Settings → Account Actions → Submit Appeal
  • Marketplace/plugin problems: → Marketplace Center → “Report Asset” or “Need Help?”
  • Sponsorships or Quest campaigns: → Campaign Dashboard → Contact tab
  • Relay/tech issues (for Pathweavers or devs): → Constellation → Admin Tools → Submit Tech Request
  • General help: → Settings → “Report a Problem”
    → https://support.noiz.gg (official external portal)

What to Avoid

🚫 Red flags — never trust:

  • “NOIZ staff” accounts on social media asking for login details
  • Users claiming you won a ⚡︎dB airdrop or “mystery boost”
  • Offers to unban, fast-track, or “verify” your account
  • People asking for cash, crypto, or gift cards in exchange for perks
  • Plugins requiring external login — always check Marketplace listing trust level

✅ Real staff:

  • Have badges visible on-platform
  • Use only official communication paths
  • Will never rush or threaten you

What If You're Unsure?

If you’re contacted by someone claiming to be NOIZ staff and something feels off:

  1. Check their profile: Badge, profile name, and join history
  2. Verify the email/domain: Only @noiz.gg or system-based inbox
  3. Use the Report Tool:
    → Category: Impersonation or Staff Spoofing

If you accidentally shared info:

  • Immediately revoke access in Constellation → Active Sessions
  • Submit a report using “Compromised Account”
  • A verified Staff Admin will respond via official platform channels

FAQ

How do I know if a staff DM is real?
Only messages from the in-app NOIZ inbox or @noiz.gg email addresses are official.
Can staff message me on social media?
No. Unless prearranged via NOIZ, this is not permitted.
What if I want to talk to a real person?
Use the in-app tools — verified staff respond directly through NOIZ channels.
Can I request a specific DCT member?
You can reference prior cases or team members in appeals, but assignments are based on case type.
