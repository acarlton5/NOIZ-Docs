The Power of Interactive Support

NOIZ is built on active, meaningful participation. Instead of relying on passive metrics like view counts, the platform rewards direct interaction — and viewer support plays a key role in that system.

Actions like gifting subs, boosting live streams, and completing quests all contribute to a creator’s visibility and progression. These actions are powered by Decibels (dB), NOIZ’s virtual support tokens, which are never framed as money or purchases.

Interactive support benefits everyone:

  • Viewers unlock cosmetic perks, badge milestones, and personal rewards
  • Creators earn ⚡︎dB, increase their Echostage eligibility, and make measurable progress
  • The system reinforces community interaction without being pay-to-win or performance-ranked

Sub Gifting: How It Works

Viewers can gift subscriptions to others on NOIZ using 500 dB per sub. This is a support action, not a purchase — it unlocks non-financial, digital perks for both the recipient and the creator.

Recipients of a gifted sub receive:

  • A sub badge, which may be time-based or custom
  • Access to exclusive emotes, overlays, audio, or channel perks (if enabled)
  • Entry into sub-gated Quests or features

Gifting subs also contributes to milestone badges, visible to the gifter. These include:

  • 5, 10, 25, 50, 100, 150, 200, 250…
  • …continuing in 1,000-point intervals up to 5,000+

Creators receive ⚡︎dB from each gifted sub. These are immediately added to their earnings, provided they meet monetization eligibility.

Gifting does not grant ownership of anything — it’s a show of support, and non-refundable once processed.

Boosting Streams

Boosts are viewer-driven visibility surges powered by Decibels or Reverb.

Boosting a live stream can:

  • Raise Echostage placement probability
  • Temporarily activate Boost Mode in chat (enhanced emotes, effects, visual density)
  • Trigger Hype Moment sequences (if collective activity thresholds are met)

Boosts are non-guaranteed — meaning:

  • They don’t directly alter payouts or guarantee spotlighting
  • They decay over time unless renewed through additional engagement

Where enabled, Reverb and Decibel boosts can combine for increased effect.

Boosting yourself is discouraged and doesn’t count toward progression systems.

Quest Bonuses

Quests are interactive challenges built into the NOIZ platform. When completed, they can trigger Quest Bonuses — additional perks for creators and viewers alike.

Bonus types may include:

  • dB rewards for viewers
  • Milestone progress for both viewer and creator
  • Temporary Echostage weight boosts, if many quests are completed quickly

Some Quest bonuses are sponsored, meaning they’re backed by verified brands or funded by NOIZ campaigns.

All Quest bonuses remain virtual and platform-contained — no fiat currency or off-platform goods are involved.

How Creators Benefit

Creators gain ⚡︎dB and increased visibility from viewer actions such as:

  • Receiving gifted subs
  • Having quests completed (if linked to their stream)
  • Being boosted during a live broadcast

These actions impact platform progression by:

  • Counting toward Partner or Partner+ tiers
  • Unlocking Vibe milestone badges
  • Influencing dashboard stats and engagement insights

Boosts, in particular, affect Echostage routing only when fueled by viewer engagement — not raw currency or automation.

There is no leaderboard or payout bonus for boosts — they are a visibility tool, not a monetization mechanic.

Boost & Gift Transparency

All support actions are visible in stream alerts and analytics dashboards unless privacy mode is enabled.

Streamers can track:

  • Top gifters
  • Boost streaks or spikes
  • Quest completions and bonus chains

Viewers cannot boost or gift anonymously unless they’ve activated privacy settings — helping keep community activity transparent and accountable.

Support vs. Purchase Reminder

It’s important to understand that NOIZ does not sell access, perks, or content.

  • Gifting, boosting, and completing quests are support actions, not transactions
  • Streamers receive ⚡︎dB as recognition, not as payment or product compensation
  • All interactions are framed as voluntary digital contributions within a virtual ecosystem

This ensures that NOIZ remains legally compliant, creator-first, and free from transactional expectations.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Can I boost my own stream?
    Technically yes, but boosts initiated by the creator using their own dB do not count toward Echostage or progression metrics.
  • Do boosts expire or decay?
    Yes. Boost influence degrades over time unless sustained by new viewer engagement or activity.
  • What’s the best way to encourage Quest bonuses?
    Keep your active Quests achievable, rotate them regularly, and encourage viewers to redeem Reverb or dB at key moments (e.g., during gameplay highs or community milestones).
  • Do viewers get anything for gifting a sub?
    Yes. Gift milestones unlock exclusive badges for gifters, and some channels may offer bonus visuals or commands for recognized supporters.
