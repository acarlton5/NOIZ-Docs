Creator Progression FAQ (Verified / Affiliate / Partner / Partner+)

What Are the Creator Progression Levels on NOIZ?

NOIZ recognizes creators through a structured tier system that reflects platform activity, support, and engagement — not vanity metrics.

  • Verified: Entry recognition for creators with consistent activity and completed onboarding
  • Affiliate: First monetized badge tier (unlocks cashout and support tools)
  • Partner: Mid-tier for creators with strong community interaction and platform presence
  • Partner+: Top-tier for long-term builders, community leaders, or official platform collaborators

Each level brings new visibility, access, and earning opportunities.

What Does “Verified” Mean?

Verification marks your channel as established and trusted. It’s typically auto-assigned after:

  • Consistent activity
  • Account completion and Constellation handshake
  • Legal identity verification

What Verified Unlocks:

  • Customization of Reverb (channel points)
  • Quest creation tools
  • Use of core moderation tools
  • Appearance in platform-wide discovery and search

Verification does not require a large audience — it’s based on real interaction and consistency.

How Do I Become a Partner?

Partner status is earned, not applied for. The system tracks your organic platform activity.

Metrics Used:

  • Streaming frequency and Quest usage
  • Viewer activity (⚡︎dB earned, Reverb redemptions, Vibe reactions)
  • Boosts and gifted subs from others
  • Adherence to maturity labels and category accuracy
  • Completion of internal platform achievements

NOIZ does not use follower count or force creators to “apply with 75 average viewers.” Growth is measured by quality and consistency.

What Do Partners Get?

  • Higher priority in Echostage rotations
  • Sponsored Quest eligibility
  • Full Marketplace participation (if Artist or Developer)
  • Early access to new tools and experiments
  • Advanced dashboard metrics (Boost trends, Quest analytics)
  • Partner badge in chat and profile

What Is Partner+?

Partner+ is a special designation for creators who:

  • Maintain long-term platform growth
  • Complete verified sponsorship or platform campaigns
  • Lead community development efforts
  • Are selected for experimental programs or early integrations

Partner+ is assigned manually or auto-granted when milestone thresholds are passed. It’s the highest visible badge level on NOIZ.

How Is Progression Tracked?

NOIZ tracks metrics internally — no public leaderboards or vanity scoreboards.

Considered Metrics:

  • ⚡︎dB earned from others (not self)
  • Chat engagement: Reverb, emotes, boosts
  • Quest participation and viewer completion rates
  • Streaming consistency (not just hours)
  • Maturity rating accuracy and content stability
  • Achievement system progression (not public unless chosen)

Can You Lose Status?

Yes — NOIZ uses a decay-based progression cycle:

  • Your Creator Level resets to Level 1 each calendar year
  • You retain your highest badge tier for the next full year, regardless of activity
  • If you don’t re-earn the requirements for that badge within the new year, you’ll drop to the highest tier you did earn

Example: You become a Partner+ in 2025. You keep the Partner+ badge and perks until the end of 2026. If in 2026 your metrics fall to Affiliate level, you’ll display Affiliate in 2027.

This helps ensure badges reflect current and active creators, not inactive legacy accounts.

Status Will Not Be Removed For:

  • Short breaks, vacations, or health issues
  • Changing content styles or exploring new categories

Status Can Be Removed For:

  • Policy violations or community harm
  • Fraudulent activity, including view botting or abuse of boosts
  • Violation of monetization rules
  • Repeated infractions after DCT or Staff Admin review

FAQ

Is there a way to apply for Partner?
No — all progression is automatic. You’ll be notified if your badge tier changes.
How long does it take to get Verified?
Typically within a few days of consistent activity and completion of onboarding steps.
Will I get an email when I level up?
Yes — all badge promotions are accompanied by a platform notification and/or email.
What’s the difference between Partner and Partner+ in day-to-day tools?
Partner+ unlocks higher priority for Echostage, more sponsorship options, and early feature access — but daily creator tools remain largely the same.
