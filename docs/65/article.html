1. What Is a Quest Campaign on NOIZ?

A Quest Campaign is a collaborative promotional event between NOIZ and an approved sponsor, game studio, or verified partner. These campaigns are designed to:

  • Feature time-limited viewer and streamer goals
  • Distribute platform-issued rewards like ⚡︎dB, digital collectibles, or overlays
  • Use in-stream plugin visibility and metadata tracking
  • Offer Echostage priority or Marketplace tie-ins (when applicable)
Quest Campaigns are not publicly creatable. All campaigns are built in collaboration with NOIZ staff to ensure platform integrity, legal compliance, and proper reward enforcement.

2. Who Can Propose a Campaign?

Eligible entities include:

  • Verified game developers or publishers
  • Approved official partners and sponsors
  • Marketplace creators launching asset packs tied to a campaign
  • Internal NOIZ teams launching seasonal or platform events

All campaign proposals are reviewed for:

  • Content alignment (tone, themes, maturity)
  • Legal and platform compliance (regional laws, reward eligibility)
  • Budget validation and reward pool logistics

3. What Sponsors Can Expect

The standard design process includes:

  1. Initial pitch meeting with NOIZ staff
  2. Agreement on:
    • Featured game or brand
    • Campaign duration and eligibility
    • Target viewer/streamer actions (e.g., watch time, ⚡︎dB use, chat interaction)
  3. Reward planning:
    • NOIZ-funded or sponsor-funded ⚡︎dB pool
    • Custom emotes, badges, overlays
    • Marketplace tie-ins or credit bundles
  4. Plugin configuration/testing
  5. Streamer onboarding (if targeted channels are included)
  6. Final approvals + co-marketing announcement

4. Permitted Reward Types

Allowed:

  • ⚡︎dB (platform-managed virtual tokens)
  • Digital collectibles: emotes, overlays, badges
  • Viewer milestones: Reverb bonuses
  • Marketplace credits

Not allowed:

  • Physical goods (unless NOIZ manages fulfillment)
  • Crypto, NFTs, or speculative tokens
  • Off-platform prizes or services
  • Gift cards or direct money equivalents

5. Plugin & Stream Behavior Requirements

All Quest Campaigns must:

  • Use the Quest Plugin (visible during stream)
  • Include clear disclosure (e.g., "Sponsored by [Game Name]")
  • Be hosted on non-AFK, chat-active streams
  • Be eligible for clip-based or audit review

Campaign participation is monitored for:

  • Accurate tagging and maturity labels
  • Streamer presence and engagement
  • Compliance with streamer and viewer behavior standards

6. Campaign Enforcement and Review

Violation of campaign or platform rules may result in:

  • Campaign removal or suspension
  • Reward revocation or rerouting
  • Blacklisting from future quests

If a streamer abuses a campaign (e.g., muted embeds, fake activity):

  • ⚡︎dB may be frozen
  • Partner/affiliate status may be reviewed
  • Quest tool access may be restricted

7. Submitting a Proposal

To propose a campaign, sponsors must:

  • Be a Verified Sponsor or invited by NOIZ staff
  • Submit a formal proposal via the Sponsor Portal or direct contact with Business Development
  • Agree to digital-only rewards and platform compliance standards
Typical turnaround time: 7–10 business days from submission to approval (varies by scope)

8. FAQ

Can I run a quest with physical merch?
No, unless NOIZ manages production and fulfillment.
Can I fund the rewards using ⚡︎dB?
Yes, we can escrow a reward pool using charged Decibels.
Can I whitelist streamers for the campaign?
Yes, with prior approval from NOIZ staff.
Will viewers know it’s sponsored?
Yes. Quests include automatic plugin labels and visible sponsor disclosures.
