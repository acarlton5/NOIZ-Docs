Referral Badges track the verified users you’ve brought to NOIZ. While these badges are invisible in chat, they qualify you for the Ambassador Badge — a visible status awarded to trusted community builders.

1. What Are Referral Badges?

Referral Badges are progress markers earned when you successfully invite people to NOIZ and they:

  • Join through your unique referral link
  • Verify their identity
  • Stream, support others, or complete quest milestones

These badges are not shown publicly — they exist to measure your community-building impact.

2. How to Refer Someone to NOIZ

Every NOIZ user has a personal referral link in:
Dashboard → Referrals → Invite

When someone:

  1. Signs up using your link
  2. Verifies their account
  3. Completes key activity milestones

…you earn progress toward your Referral Badge tiers.

3. Referral Badge Tiers (Invisible)

TierRequirement
🥈 Bronze Recruiter3 referred users (verified)
🥇 Silver Advocate10 referred users
💎 Gold Builder25 referred users
🚀 Ecosystem Ally50 referred users with milestone activity
🛡️ Platform Enabler100+ referrals with monetization unlocks

Note: These tiers do not appear in chat — they are internal progress milestones toward Ambassador eligibility.

4. What Is the Ambassador Badge?

The Ambassador Badge is public and visible:

  • Appears next to your name in all chats
  • Shows on your profile page
  • Displays in leaderboards and clips

You earn it by:

  • Reaching and fully liquidating a set number of Referral Badges (threshold set by NOIZ)
  • Passing a platform approval check for:
  • Positive support history
  • No referral spam or abuse
  • Ongoing good standing with NOIZ

🚫 Disqualification: Referral farming, alt account abuse, or automated mass invites result in a permanent loss of eligibility.

5. How to Track Referral Progress

From Dashboard → Referrals, you can see:

  • Total users referred
  • Which referrals verified and reached milestones
  • Your current Referral Badge tier
  • Your Ambassador status: Pending / Approved / Denied

6. Why Referral Badges Matter

Referral Badges:

  • Recognize real community growth
  • Highlight Ambassadors during platform quests or events
  • Prioritize eligible users for:
  • Early feature access
  • Beta tests
  • Sponsored campaigns

Ambassadors may receive:

  • Exclusive cosmetics
  • Early Marketplace listing access
  • Invitations to private feedback quests for new features

7. FAQ

Can I see who used my referral link?
→ Yes — via anonymized stats, not full usernames

Can streamers offer extra incentives for using their link?
→ No — offering external rewards counts as abuse and disqualifies both parties

Is Ambassador status permanent?
→ Yes, unless revoked for policy violations

Can I re-earn Ambassador if I lose it?
→ No — revocation is permanent
